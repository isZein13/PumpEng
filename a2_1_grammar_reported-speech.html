Reported Speech (Косвенная речь)

Упражнения на преобразование прямой речи в косвенную для Present Simple и Past Simple.

Reported Speech (Косвенная речь) используется для передачи слов другого человека. Основное правило - "шаг назад во времени" (one step back).

  • Present Simple → Past Simple: "I like tea." → He said that he liked tea.
  • Past Simple → Past Perfect: "I bought a car." → She said that she had bought a car.
  • Местоимения также меняются: "I like..." → He said that he liked...

Упражнение 1: Present Simple в косвенной речи

1. Direct: "I work in a hospital." → She said that she ___ in a hospital.

2. Direct: "They like pizza." → He said that they ___ pizza.

3. Direct: "He plays the guitar." → She told me that he ___ the guitar.

4. Direct: "We live in a small flat." → They said that they ___ in a small flat.

5. Direct: "I don't like coffee." → He said that he ___ coffee.

Упражнение 2: Past Simple в косвенной речи

1. Direct: "I visited Paris last year." → He said that he ___ Paris the year before.

2. Direct: "She bought a new dress." → He told me that she ___ a new dress.

3. Direct: "We saw a great film." → They said that they ___ a great film.

4. Direct: "He didn't come to the party." → She said that he ___ to the party.

5. Direct: "I made a mistake." → I admitted that I ___ a mistake.
