Modal Verbs of Deduction

Упражнения на выражение предположений и логических выводов о настоящем.

Модальные глаголы могут использоваться для выражения того, насколько мы уверены в чем-либо. После них всегда идет глагол в начальной форме (V1).

  • must + V1: Сильная уверенность (≈95%), что что-то является правдой. (The lights are on. He must be home. - Должно быть, он дома.)
  • may / might / could + V1: Неуверенность, предположение (≈50%), что что-то возможно. (He isn't answering. He might be busy. - Возможно, он занят.)
  • can't + V1: Сильная уверенность (≈95%), что что-то НЕ является правдой; невозможно. (He just left 5 minutes ago. He can't be at work yet. - Не может быть, что он уже на работе.)

Упражнение 1: 'must' (Сильная уверенность)

Используйте 'must', когда вы почти уверены, что это правда.

1. The lights are on in their house. They ___ be at home.

2. He's been working for 12 hours. He ___ be very tired.

3. She's not answering her phone. She ___ be busy.

4. You haven't eaten all day. You ___ be hungry.

5. He has a huge library. He ___ love reading.

6. The ground is all wet. It ___ have rained last night.

7. Sarah knows so much about Paris. She ___ have lived there.

8. He's wearing a doctor's uniform. He ___ work at the hospital.

9. This ___ be the right address. The GPS says so.

10. Everyone is laughing. The joke ___ be very funny.

Упражнение 2: 'can't' (Сильная уверенность в обратном)

Используйте 'can't', когда вы почти уверены, что это неправда.

1. You just had a big lunch. You ___ be hungry already.

2. He said he was in London today, but I just saw him here. It ___ be true.

3. She's afraid of heights. She ___ be a pilot.

4. They bought a new car last week. They ___ need another one.

5. He doesn't speak French at all. That ___ be his brother on the phone speaking perfect French.

6. This is a five-star hotel. It ___ be cheap.

7. You've been sleeping all day. You ___ be tired.

8. He has a test tomorrow. He ___ be serious about going to a party tonight.

9. This steak is cold and tough. It ___ be fresh.

10. She's a vegetarian. She ___ be eating a hamburger.

Упражнение 3: 'may / might / could' (Возможность)

Используйте 'may', 'might' или 'could', когда вы не уверены.

1. "Where's Jane?" "I'm not sure. She ___ be in the garden."

2. Don't eat those mushrooms. They ___ be poisonous.

3. I don't know if I will go out tonight. I ___ just stay home.

4. This ___ be the house we're looking for, but I can't see the number.

5. Don't throw that ticket away. It ___ be important later.

6. There's someone at the door. It ___ be the postman.

7. I'm not sure what to do. I ___ apply for the job, or I ___ not.

8. This isn't her signature, but I'm not certain. It ___ be a fake.

9. He ___ look calm, but I'm sure he is very nervous inside.

10. The phone is ringing. It ___ be Sarah, she said she would call.

Упражнение 4: Смешанная практика (must, can't, might)

1. I can't find my keys anywhere. I ___ have left them at work.

2. That woman ___ be a doctor. She's been helping the injured man.

3. He lives in a huge house and drives a Ferrari. He ___ have a lot of money.

4. You're going to a warm country. It ___ be cold there, so you don't need a winter coat.

5. The boss wants to see you. It ___ be about the new project. Or maybe something else.

6. You got the best grade in the class! You ___ be so proud of yourself.

7. He looks nothing like his brother. They ___ be twins.

8. This painting ___ be a real Picasso. It would be in a museum, not here.

9. Don't stand under that old tree. A branch ___ fall on your head.

10. He's been coughing all day. He ___ be getting sick.

Упражнение 5: Выбери логичный ответ

Выберите наиболее логичное продолжение диалога.

1. A: David isn't at home. B: Well, he ___ be at the gym. He sometimes goes there after work.

2. A: Look, this designer bag is only $10! B: It ___ be real. Designer bags are very expensive.

3. A: Someone is knocking at the door. B: That ___ be Jane. She said she would come around this time.

4. A: I don't know this word. B: Let me see... I'm not sure, but it ___ mean "difficult".

5. A: Why is he so happy? B: He ___ have heard some good news.

6. A: She didn't wave back. B: She ___ have seen you. She wasn't wearing her glasses.

7. A: The restaurant is completely empty. B: It ___ be very good, then.

8. A: I have a missed call from an unknown number. B: It ___ be important. You should probably call back.

9. A: The cat has been sleeping all day. B: It ___ be feeling unwell.

10. A: My keys are not on the table. B: You ___ have put them in your bag. Check there.
