Conditionals (Types 2 & 3)

Упражнения на нереальные условия в настоящем, будущем и прошлом.

На уровне B1 мы углубляемся в нереальные условные предложения, которые описывают воображаемые ситуации и их последствия.

  • Conditional Type 2 (Нереальное настоящее/будущее): If + Past Simple, ... would + V1.
    • Используется для гипотез и советов: If I were you, I would talk to him. (На твоем месте я бы с ним поговорил.)
  • Conditional Type 3 (Нереальное прошлое): If + Past Perfect, ... would have + V3.
    • Используется для сожалений о прошлом и размышлений о том, что могло бы быть: If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam. (Если бы я учился усерднее, я бы сдал экзамен.)

Упражнение 1: Second Conditional (Review)

1. If I ___ (know) her secret, I would tell you.

2. What would you do if you ___ (see) a ghost?

3. If he ___ (be) more careful, he wouldn't make so many mistakes.

4. I ___ (not / say) that if I were you.

5. If we ___ (have) more time, we could visit the museum.

6. Where ___ you ___ (live) if you could choose any country?

7. If I ___ (find) a wallet in the street, I would take it to the police.

8. The world would be a better place if everyone ___ (help) each other.

9. If my car ___ (not be) so old, I would drive across the country.

10. He ___ (be) happier if he ___ (not complain) so much.

Упражнение 2: Third Conditional

1. If I ___ (study) harder, I would have passed the exam.

2. We wouldn't have missed the train if we ___ (leave) earlier.

3. If she ___ (not / forget) her passport, she would have been on the plane.

4. I ___ (call) you if my phone hadn't died.

5. If you ___ (tell) me about the party, I would have come.

6. The accident ___ (not / happen) if the driver had been more careful.

7. If I ___ (know) you were coming, I would have baked a cake.

8. They ___ (win) the match if their best player hadn't been injured.

9. If we ___ (not / spend) all our money, we could have stayed longer.

10. What ___ you ___ (do) if you had missed your flight?

Упражнение 4: Second или Third Conditional?

Проанализируйте ситуацию и выберите правильный тип условного предложения.

1. I don't have a car, but if I ___ one, I would drive you to the airport.

2. We missed the flight yesterday. If we ___ earlier, we would have caught it.

3. I'm not a doctor. If I ___ a doctor, I would help people.

4. I didn't see you at the party last night. If I ___ you, I would have said hello.

5. What would you do if you ___ a famous celebrity for a day?

6. The team lost the game on Saturday. They ___ if they had trained harder.

7. I'm busy tomorrow. If I ___ free, I would join you.

8. He didn't know about the meeting. If someone ___ him, he would have come.

9. If I spoke French fluently, I ___ for that job in Paris.

10. The weather was terrible. We ___ for a picnic if it had been sunny.

Упражнение 5: Трансформация из реальной ситуации

Выберите правильное условное предложение, которое описывает нереальную альтернативу.

1. I'm not a bird, so I can't fly.

2. I didn't study, so I failed the test.

3. I don't have enough money, so I can't buy that dress.

4. It rained yesterday, so we didn't go to the beach.

5. I can't speak Japanese, so I can't work in Tokyo.

6. He was busy, so he didn't come with us.

7. I don't know the answer, so I can't tell you.

8. She missed the bus, so she was late.

9. The internet isn't working, so I can't check my email.

10. I didn't have your email address, so I didn't send you the file.
