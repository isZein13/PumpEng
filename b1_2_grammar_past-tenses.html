Past Tenses (Review & Comparison)

Углубленная практика и сравнение Past Simple, Past Continuous и Present Perfect.

На уровне B1 ключевым навыком является умение различать прошедшие времена в контексте повествования.

  • Past Simple используется для завершенных действий в прошлом, часто с указанием времени (yesterday, last week, in 2010).
  • Past Continuous используется для описания процесса в определенный момент в прошлом или как фоновое действие для другого, более короткого события.
  • Present Perfect используется для действий в прошлом, которые имеют результат или связь с настоящим, а также для жизненного опыта без указания точного времени.

Упражнение 1: Past Simple (Review)

Вспомните формы Past Simple для правильных и неправильных глаголов.

1. I ___ (visit) my grandparents last weekend.

2. She ___ (buy) a new dress yesterday.

3. They ___ (not / go) to the cinema on Friday.

4. ___ you ___ (see) the match last night?

5. He ___ (write) an email to his boss two hours ago.

6. We ___ (live) in that house from 2005 to 2015.

7. The film ___ (not / be) very good.

8. Where ___ your parents ___ (go) on holiday last year?

9. I ___ (find) my keys under the sofa.

10. The Roman Empire ___ (fall) a long time ago.

Упражнение 2: Past Continuous (Review)

Вспомните использование Past Continuous для процессов в прошлом.

1. At 7 PM yesterday, I ___ (have) dinner with my family.

2. What were you doing when I ___ (call) you?

3. It ___ (rain) when I left the office.

4. While my parents ___ (watch) TV, I was doing my homework.

5. He ___ (not / sleep) at midnight; he was still studying.

6. The sun ___ (shine) and the birds were singing.

7. ___ you ___ (work) at this time last Friday?

8. As I ___ (walk) down the street, I saw an old friend.

9. He hurt his back while he ___ (lift) a heavy box.

10. They ___ (not / pay) attention, so they didn't see the sign.

Упражнение 3: Present Perfect (Review)

Вспомните случаи употребления Present Perfect.

1. I can't find my wallet. I think I ___ (lose) it.

2. ___ you ever ___ (be) to Japan?

3. She ___ (live) in this city since 2015.

4. We ___ (not / see) this film yet.

5. He ___ (just / finish) his lunch.

6. I ___ (already / read) that book.

7. They ___ (know) each other for ten years.

8. My brother ___ (never / travel) by plane.

9. ___ you ___ (speak) to your boss today?

10. The weather ___ (change). It's much colder now.

Упражнение 4: Past Simple vs. Present Perfect

1. I ___ (not see) him since last month.

2. They ___ (get) married ten years ago.

3. Albert Einstein ___ (be) a scientist who developed the theory of relativity.

4. The team ___ (win) the championship. They are celebrating now.

5. "___ you ___ (finish) your work yet?" "Yes, I ___ (finish) it an hour ago."

6. My grandfather is a writer. He ___ (write) more than twenty books.

7. I ___ (not / eat) sushi before. This is my first time.

8. What time ___ you ___ (get) home last night?

9. It ___ (rain) a lot this week. The ground is still wet.

10. I ___ (live) in Moscow for two years, from 2018 to 2020.

Упражнение 5: Past Simple vs. Past Continuous

2. I ___ when you ___ me last night.

3. They ___ television when the power ___.

4. As she ___ home, she ___ her old teacher.

5. He ___ his keys while he ___.

6. When I ___ at the party, everyone ___.

7. I ___ a book when I suddenly ___ a strange noise.

8. While the children ___, their mother ___ dinner.

9. He ___ his ankle while he ___.

10. The police ___ him while he ___.
