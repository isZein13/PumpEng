Causative Form (have/get something done)

Упражнения на конструкции, которые показывают, что действие выполнено кем-то другим для нас.

Causative Form используется, когда мы не сами выполняем действие, а организуем, чтобы его выполнил кто-то другой (обычно профессионал за плату). Основная структура:

  • have + object + Past Participle (V3)
    • I had my car repaired. (Мне починили машину. - Не я сам, а механик.)
  • get + object + Past Participle (V3) (более неформальный вариант)
    • I need to get my hair cut. (Мне нужно подстричься. - Не я сам, а парикмахер.)
  • Эта конструкция может использоваться в разных временах, при этом меняется только глагол `have` или `get`.

Упражнение 1: Образование с 'have'

1. I need to have my photo ___. (take)

2. She had her watch ___ yesterday. (repair)

3. We are having our house ___ at the moment. (paint)

4. He has his suits ___ by a tailor. (make)

5. I must have my computer ___. It's very slow. (fix)

6. They will have their new kitchen ___ next month. (install)

7. She has her food ___ to her office every day. (deliver)

8. I have just had my eyes ___. (test)

9. We had the carpets ___ last week. (clean)

10. You should have your hair ___. It looks great. (cut)

Упражнение 2: Образование с 'get'

1. I'm going to get my car ___ tomorrow. (wash)

2. She got her passport photo ___ last week. (take)

3. You need to get that broken window ___. (replace)

4. We get our newspaper ___ every morning. (deliver)

5. I can't believe he got his name ___ on his arm! (tattoo)

6. She finally got her first book ___. (publish)

7. We need to get this document ___ by a professional. (translate)

8. He got his apartment ___ while he was on vacation. (burgle)

9. I need to get my suit ___ for the wedding. (clean)

10. They got a new security system ___ last month. (install)

Упражнение 3: Трансформация предложений

Выберите правильное каузативное предложение, которое соответствует исходному.

1. A photographer is taking a picture of me. → I am ___.

2. A mechanic repaired her car. → She ___.

3. The hairdresser will cut my hair. → I ___.

4. Someone is painting their house. → They are ___.

5. A dentist checks my teeth every year. → I ___ every year.

6. Someone stole his phone. → He ___.

7. A company will deliver our new sofa. → We will ___.

8. The optician has tested her eyes. → She ___.

9. A professional is cleaning our carpets. → We are ___.

10. The dry-cleaner cleaned my suit. → I ___.

Упражнение 4: Смешанная практика времен

Выберите правильную форму каузативной конструкции в нужном времени.

1. I usually ___ my car serviced once a year. (Present Simple)

2. She ___ her hair cut last week. (Past Simple)

3. We ___ a new security system installed tomorrow. (Future Simple)

4. He ___ his wisdom tooth removed yesterday. (Past Simple)

5. I ___ my passport renewed. Now I can travel. (Present Perfect)

6. They ___ their house painted at the moment. (Present Continuous)

7. I need to ___ this document translated into English. (Infinitive)

8. She ___ her photo taken for her new ID card. (Past Continuous)

9. We ___ our windows cleaned next month. (Future with 'be going to')

10. You should ___ your eyes tested regularly. (Modal verb)

Упражнение 5: Causative или Active?

Выберите правильный вариант в зависимости от того, кто выполняет действие.

1. I ___ my hair every morning. (I do it myself)

2. I ___ every week. (The cleaner does it)

3. He ___ yesterday. (He did it himself)

4. She ___ last month. (A professional did it)

5. We are not cooking tonight. We ___. (A restaurant is delivering it)

6. Be careful! I ___ just ___. (I did it myself)

7. My watch wasn't working, so I ___. (I took it to a specialist)

8. They built a new fence themselves. They ___.

9. Are you going to ___ or are you going to get someone to do it? (You yourself)

10. I didn't recognize her because she ___. (A hairdresser did it)
