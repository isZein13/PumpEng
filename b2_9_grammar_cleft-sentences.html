Cleft Sentences (Эмфатические конструкции)

Упражнения на конструкции, которые используются для выделения определенной части предложения.

Cleft Sentences — это способ перестроить предложение, чтобы сделать акцент на определенной информации. Основных типов два:

  • It-clefts: Структура `It + be + [выделяемая часть] + that/who...`
    • Обычное предложение: John broke the window.
    • С акцентом на "John": It was John who broke the window.
    • С акцентом на "the window": It was the window that John broke.
  • Wh-clefts: Структура `What/Where/Why/The reason why... + clause + be + [выделяемая часть]`
    • Обычное предложение: I need a cup of tea.
    • С акцентом на "a cup of tea": What I need is a cup of tea.
    • С акцентом на причину: The reason why I came is to talk to you.

Упражнение 1: 'It'-clefts

1. ___ my brother who called you, not me.

2. It was the film ___ we watched last night.

3. ___ yesterday that I realized my mistake.

4. It is Sarah ___ speaks fluent Japanese.

5. Was it ___ you were talking to?

6. It's on Fridays ___ we usually have our team meetings.

7. ___ the red car that was parked illegally, not the blue one.

8. It was the manager ___ made the final decision.

9. It's because he was ill ___ he didn't come to work.

10. Was it last year ___ you went to China?

Упражнение 2: 'Wh'-clefts (with 'What')

1. ___ I need right now is a long holiday.

2. ___ he said was very surprising.

3. ___ I like about this city is its beautiful parks.

4. ___ happened was that we missed our flight.

5. ___ we need to do is find a solution quickly.

6. ___ surprised me was his reaction.

7. ___ you're saying doesn't make any sense.

8. ___ I find most difficult about learning English is the pronunciation.

9. ___ I want to know is why you didn't call me.

10. ___ matters most is that you are safe.

Упражнение 3: Other 'Wh'-clefts

1. ___ I grew up was a small town by the sea.

2. ___ I called you was to ask for some advice.

3. ___ we always meet is at that little cafe on the corner.

4. ___ I remember most vividly is the day we graduated.

5. ___ she was so upset was that she had lost her favourite necklace.

6. ___ you need to park your car is just around the corner.

7. ___ he finally proposed to her was on a bridge in Paris.

8. ___ he's so successful is because he works incredibly hard.

9. ___ I'll never forget is the kindness she showed me.

10. ___ we should meet is at the main entrance.

Упражнение 4: 'It'-clefts vs. 'Wh'-clefts

Выберите наилучшую конструкцию для выделения информации.

1. To emphasize "his attitude": His attitude annoys me.

2. To emphasize "a new car": I need a new car.

3. To emphasize "yesterday": He broke the vase yesterday.

4. To emphasize "the loud music": The loud music is giving me a headache.

5. To emphasize "Mary": Mary won the prize.

6. To emphasize "his honesty": I admire his honesty the most.

7. To emphasize "the blue one": I want the blue one.

8. To emphasize "in the morning": She feels most creative in the morning.

9. To emphasize "the final exam": The final exam is causing me stress.

10. To emphasize "the reason": The reason is that I was ill.

Упражнение 5: Rephrasing Sentences

Выберите лучший способ перефразировать предложение с помощью Cleft Sentence.

1. He broke the window. (Emphasize "the window")

2. I miss her smile the most. (Emphasize "her smile")

3. She bought the car from Tom. (Emphasize "Tom")

4. We need more time to finish the project. (Emphasize "more time")

5. He left because he was angry. (Emphasize the reason)

6. I saw him in the park. (Emphasize "the park")

7. Her voice is what I find so irritating. (Rephrase with 'It'-cleft)

8. They got married in June. (Emphasize "in June")

9. He is worried about his final exam results. (Emphasize "final exam results")

10. The lack of communication caused the problem. (Emphasize "The lack of communication")
