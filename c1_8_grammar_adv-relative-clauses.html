Advanced Relative Clauses

Упражнения на сложные случаи употребления относительных предложений, включая предлоги и `whom`.

На уровне C1 мы оттачиваем использование относительных предложений, фокусируясь на формальных и стилистических нюансах.

  • `who` vs. `whom`: `who` — подлежащее (совершает действие), `whom` — дополнение (действие совершается над ним). В формальной речи `whom` используется после предлогов. The man who called... vs. The man to whom I spoke...
  • `which` referring to a whole clause: `which` может относиться не к одному слову, а ко всей предыдущей идее. He arrived late, which annoyed everyone.
  • Prepositions in Relative Clauses: Предлог может стоять в конце (неформально) или перед `whom`/`which` (формально).
    • The person who I was talking to... (Informal)
    • The person to whom I was talking... (Formal)
  • Omitting the Relative Pronoun: Местоимение можно опустить, если оно является дополнением в defining clause. The book (that/which) I'm reading is great.

Упражнение 1: 'who' vs. 'whom' vs. 'whose'

Выберите наиболее подходящее местоимение.

1. The manager, ___ I met yesterday, was very helpful.

2. This is the artist ___ work I admire greatly.

3. The woman to ___ I was speaking is a famous scientist.

4. I have several friends ___ live abroad.

5. The students, most of ___ had studied hard, passed the test.

6. Is he the person ___ you were telling me about?

7. The author, ___ latest novel is a bestseller, will be giving a talk.

8. He's a man ___ I respect immensely.

9. Do you know the girl ___ car is parked over there?

10. He is the candidate ___ we believe is best for the job.

Упражнение 2: 'which' referring to a whole clause

Выберите 'which' для комментирования всей предыдущей идеи.

1. He forgot my birthday, ___ upset me a lot.

2. The team played very well, ___ was a pleasant surprise.

3. She offered to help me with my work, ___ was very kind of her.

4. He managed to fix the car himself, ___ I found very impressive.

5. They cancelled the concert at the last minute, ___ meant we had a wasted journey.

6. My friend has decided to move to Australia, ___ is a shame as I'll miss her.

7. It rained all weekend, ___ forced us to stay indoors.

8. He passed all his exams with top marks, ___ is not surprising given how hard he studied.

9. The flight was delayed for three hours, ___ gave us time to have a proper meal.

10. She speaks five languages fluently, ___ I find truly admirable.

Упражнение 3: Prepositions in Relative Clauses

Выберите наиболее подходящий вариант (формальный или неформальный).

1. The woman with ___ I was travelling is my aunt. (Formal)

2. This is the project ___ which we have worked so hard. (Formal)

3. Who was that person you were talking ___? (Informal)

4. The hotel at ___ we stayed was excellent. (Formal)

5. This is the subject ___ I'm most interested in. (Informal)

6. The people ___ whom we had dinner were very charming. (Formal)

7. The chair ___ you are sitting on is broken. (Informal)

8. The manager to ___ you should address your complaint is on holiday. (Formal)

9. The tools with ___ he fixed the car were very old. (Formal)

10. The topic ___ we are going to discuss is very important. (Informal)

Упражнение 4: Omitting the Relative Pronoun

Выберите "Can be omitted", если относительное местоимение можно опустить.

1. The book (that) I'm reading is fascinating.

2. The man who lives next door is an architect.

3. The keys (which) you were looking for are on the table.

4. My brother, who is a doctor, lives in London.

5. The restaurant where we had dinner was closed.

6. I didn't like the film (that) we saw last night.

7. The woman whose car was stolen called the police.

8. The people (who) I work with are very friendly.

9. The politician who gave the speech was very charismatic.

10. Paris, which is the capital of France, is a beautiful city.

Упражнение 5: Mixed Advanced Practice

Выберите лучший вариант для завершения предложения.

1. He finally passed his driving test, ___ was a great relief for his parents.

2. This is the village in ___ my family has lived for generations.

3. The author, ___ books have sold millions of copies, remains very private.

4. I couldn't understand the reason ___ he was so angry.

5. The candidates ___ the company interviewed were all highly qualified.

6. He is one of the few politicians ___ I genuinely trust.

7. We went to the same school, ___ is why we know each other so well.

8. The system by ___ the winners are selected is very complex.

9. The moment ___ she walked into the room, everyone fell silent.

10. The employees, all of ___ had received a bonus, were very motivated.
