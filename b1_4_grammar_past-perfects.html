Past Perfect Simple & Continuous

Упражнения на предпрошедшее время для описания последовательности событий в прошлом.

Past Perfect Tenses используются для описания действий, которые произошли до другого момента или действия в прошлом.

  • Past Perfect Simple (had + V3): Указывает на завершенное действие или результат к моменту в прошлом.
    • When I arrived, the film had started. (Фильм начался до того, как я приехал).
  • Past Perfect Continuous (had been + V-ing): Указывает на длительность процесса до момента в прошлом. Часто объясняет причину какого-либо состояния.
    • He was tired because he had been working all night. (Он устал, потому что работал всю ночь).

Упражнение 1: Past Perfect Simple (had + V3)

1. By the time we arrived, he ___ (finish) his work.

2. She told me she ___ (never / see) such a beautiful sunset before.

3. The train ___ (already / leave) when we got to the station.

4. I couldn't buy the tickets because I ___ (forget) my wallet.

5. After they ___ (eat) dinner, they went for a walk.

6. He ___ (not / study) French before he moved to Paris.

7. When I saw her, I realized I ___ (meet) her before.

8. The film was much better than I ___ (expect).

9. ___ you ___ (book) the hotel before you went on holiday?

10. By the age of 25, she ___ (already / visit) five continents.

Упражнение 2: Past Perfect Continuous (had been + V-ing)

1. He was tired because he ___ (work) all day.

2. They ___ (drive) for hours when they finally saw the city.

3. Her eyes were red. It was clear she ___. (cry)

4. How long ___ you ___ (wait) before the bus arrived?

5. I ___ (not / feel) well for weeks before I went to the doctor.

6. She was an expert because she ___ (study) the subject since she was a teenager.

7. The ground was wet because it ___. (rain)

8. They ___ (look) for a house for six months before they found one they liked.

9. He finally passed his driving test after he ___ (take) lessons for a year.

10. I was angry because I ___ (wait) for him for over an hour.

Упражнение 3: Simple vs. Continuous

Выберите правильную форму Past Perfect: Simple (результат) или Continuous (процесс).

1. I ___ (read) the book, so I knew how the movie would end.

2. His English was excellent. He ___ (live) in London for ten years.

3. She was exhausted because she ___ (clean) the house all morning.

4. By the time I arrived, they ___ (already / eat) all the pizza.

5. They felt healthy on holiday because they ___ (walk) a lot every day.

6. He ___ (finish) three projects before the deadline.

7. I was late for the meeting because I ___ (look) for a parking space for 30 minutes.

8. She knew all about the plan because she ___ (listen) to their conversation.

9. He couldn't play in the match because he ___ (break) his leg.

10. My hands were covered in paint because I ___ the walls.

Упражнение 4: Past Perfect vs. Past Simple

Выберите правильное время: Past Perfect (предшествующее действие) или Past Simple (последующее действие).

1. When I ___ (arrive) home, my family ___ (already / finish) dinner.

2. She ___ (not want) to see the film because she ___ (read) the book.

3. I ___ (feel) much better after I ___ (take) the medicine.

4. He ___ (tell) me he ___ (not see) her for months.

5. By the time the police ___ (get) there, the robbers ___ (disappear).

6. After the guests ___ (leave), we ___ (start) to clean up.

7. I ___ (realize) that I ___ (make) a huge mistake.

8. They ___ (not know) where to go because they ___ (not bring) a map.

9. Before she ___ (visit) London, she ___ (read) a lot about it.

10. The house was dirty because nobody ___ (clean) it for weeks.

Упражнение 5: All Past Tenses Mixed

Завершите историю, выбрав правильное прошедшее время.

1. Yesterday I ___ (decide) to visit my friend, Mark. When I ___ (arrive) at his house, he wasn't there.

2. I ___ (wait) for a few minutes when I ___ (see) his neighbour.

3. She told me that Mark ___ (go) to the supermarket.

4. I felt a bit annoyed because I ___ (call) him before I left my house.

5. While I ___ (stand) there, it ___ (start) to rain.

6. I didn't have an umbrella because I ___ (not / check) the weather forecast.

7. Suddenly, my phone ___. It was Mark.

8. He apologized and said he completely ___ (forget) about our meeting.

9. He said he ___ (shop) for a new video game.

10. In the end, we ___ (meet) at a cafe and ___ (have) a good laugh about it.
