Advanced Passive Voice & Causative

Упражнения на безличные пассивные конструкции, пассивный инфинитив и герундий.

На уровне C1 мы осваиваем более формальные и стилистически сложные пассивные конструкции.

  • Impersonal Passive (Безличный пассив): Используется с reporting verbs (say, think, believe, know, expect) для передачи мнений без указания автора.
    • It is believed that the treasure is hidden nearby.
    • The treasure is believed to be hidden nearby.
  • Passive Infinitive (to be + V3): Используется после глаголов, прилагательных и модальных глаголов. The project needs to be finished. She seems to be respected by her colleagues.
  • Passive Gerund (being + V3): Используется после предлогов и глаголов, требующих герундий. He avoids being photographed. I'm tired of being told what to do.
  • Causative `have/get sth done` (Advanced): Также используется для описания неприятных событий, которые с нами произошли. I had my wallet stolen last week.

Упражнение 2: Impersonal Passive Structures

1. Active: People say he is a genius. → Passive: He ___ to be a genius.

2. Active: They believe the company made a huge profit. → Passive: It ___ that the company made a huge profit.

3. Active: People think the minister lied. → Passive: The minister ___ to have lied.

4. Active: The newspapers reported that the celebrity got married in secret. → Passive: It ___ that...

5. Active: Everyone knows she is a fantastic singer. → Passive: She ___ to be a fantastic singer.

6. Active: They expect that the team will win. → Passive: The team ___ to win.

7. Active: People consider him a genius. → Passive: He ___ a genius.

8. Active: We understand that you are busy. → Passive: It ___ that you are busy.

9. Active: They claim that this medicine cures everything. → Passive: This medicine ___ to cure everything.

10. Active: The police alleged that he was driving too fast. → Passive: He ___ to have been driving too fast.
