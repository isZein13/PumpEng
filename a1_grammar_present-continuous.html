Present Continuous (Настоящее длительное время)

Упражнения на действия, происходящие сейчас, временные ситуации и запланированные будущие события.

Present Continuous (am/is/are + V-ing) используется для:

  • Действий, происходящих **в момент речи** (I am talking now.)
  • Временных ситуаций, происходящих **в настоящий период времени** (She is living in Paris this year.)
  • Запланированных **будущих действий**, особенно с указанием времени/места (We are meeting at 6 PM tomorrow.)
  • Описания изменяющихся ситуаций (The climate is changing.)

Отрицания: am/is/are + not + V-ing (He isn't sleeping.)
Вопросы: Am/Is/Are + подлежащее + V-ing? (Are they watching TV?)

Упражнение 1: Действия в момент речи - Выберите правильную форму

1. Look! The dog ___ (play) in the park.

2. Listen! My sister ___ (sing) a song.

3. I ___ (do) my homework right now.

4. They ___ (watch) a funny cartoon at the moment.

5. Please be quiet! The baby ___ (sleep).

6. We ___ (have) lunch now.

7. You ___ (learn) English at the moment.

8. He ___ (read) a newspaper in the living room.

9. The birds ___ (fly) in the sky.

10. She ___ (wear) a beautiful dress today.

Упражнение 2: Запланированные будущие действия

1. We ___ (visit) our grandparents next Sunday.

2. I ___ (meet) my friends after school today.

3. He ___ (fly) to Paris tomorrow morning.

4. They ___ (have) a party next weekend.

5. She ___ (start) her new job on Monday.

6. What time ___ you ___ (leave) tomorrow?

7. My parents ___ (come) to see us this evening.

8. He ___ (not / work) next week. He's on holiday.

9. I ___ (go) to the dentist on Wednesday at 3 PM.

10. ___ they ___ (travel) to Spain next month?

Упражнение 3: Отрицания (am not, isn't, aren't)

1. I ___ (not / sleep) now. I am studying.

2. She ___ (not / watch) TV at the moment. She is reading.

3. We ___ (not / go) to the party tonight. We are tired.

4. Look! He ___ (not / play) with the other children.

5. They ___ (not / eat) dinner now. They are talking.

6. You ___ (not / listen) to me!

7. The computer ___ (not / work) properly at the moment.

8. My mom ___ (not / cook) dinner tonight, we are eating out.

9. I ___ (not / wear) a coat because it's warm.

10. The students ___ (not / write) an essay now, they are discussing it.

Упражнение 4: Вопросы (Am I...? Is he/she/it...? Are you/we/they...?)

1. ___ you ___ (watch) TV now?

2. What ___ he ___ (do) at the moment?

3. ___ it ___ (rain) outside?

4. Why ___ they ___ (laugh)?

5. ___ I ___ (speak) too fast?

6. Where ___ she ___ (go) now?

7. ___ we ___ (study) this topic next lesson? (план)

8. Who ___ (talk) on the phone?

9. ___ your parents ___ (work) today?

10. What book ___ you ___ (read) these days? (временная ситуация)

Упражнение 6: Present Simple или Present Continuous? (Часть 1)

1. My cat usually ___ (sleep) in his bed, but now he ___ (sleep) on the sofa.

2. "___ you ___ (understand) this rule?" "Not really, I ___ (still / think) about it."

3. He ___ (work) as a doctor. He ___ (help) a patient right now.

4. They often ___ (go) to the mountains, but this year they ___ (travel) to the sea.

5. What ___ your father ___ (do) for a living? (профессия)

6. I ___ (not / usually / watch) horror films, but this one ___ (look) interesting.

7. The children ___ (be) very quiet. What ___ they ___ (do)?

8. She ___ (study) for her exams these days. She ___ (want) to get a good grade.

9. How often ___ you ___ (visit) your cousins?

10. It ___ (get) dark. I think I ___ (go) home now. (одно - наблюдение, второе - спонтанное решение)

Упражнение 7: Present Simple или Present Continuous? (Часть 2)

1. A: What are you doing? B: I ___ for my keys.

2. She ___ coffee every morning.

3. "Be quiet! The teacher ___."

4. How often ___ you ___ your grandparents?

5. I ___ a new language this semester. (временная ситуация)

6. My train ___ at 10:30 tomorrow. (расписание)

7. What ___ that noise? Something ___ strange.

8. We ___ to a concert next Friday. We have tickets! (план)

9. He never ___ his keys. He is very organized.

10. "Why ___ you ___ a coat? It's not cold."
