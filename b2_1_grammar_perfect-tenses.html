Perfect Tenses (Advanced)

Упражнения на глубокое понимание и сравнение всех совершенных времен.

На уровне B2 мы оттачиваем использование всех шести "совершенных" времен, фокусируясь на тонких различиях в значении.

  • Present Perfect Simple (have done): Акцент на результате или количестве. I've written three emails.
  • Present Perfect Continuous (have been doing): Акцент на длительности процесса. I've been writing emails all morning.
  • Past Perfect Simple (had done): Завершенное действие до другого момента в прошлом. He had left when I arrived.
  • Past Perfect Continuous (had been doing): Длительный процесс до другого момента в прошлом. He was tired because he had been driving for hours.
  • Future Perfect Simple (will have done): Действие завершится к моменту в будущем. I will have finished by 5 PM.
  • Future Perfect Continuous (will have been doing): Как долго будет длиться процесс к моменту в будущем. By 5 PM, I will have been working for eight hours.

Упражнение 1: Present Perfect Simple vs. Continuous

1. I'm exhausted! I ___ (work) in the garden all day.

2. She ___ (read) three books this week.

3. How long ___ you ___ (wait) for me?

4. I ___ (know) him since we were children.

5. They ___ (watch) that series for hours.

6. We ___ (paint) the kitchen, and we're only halfway through.

7. Look! I ___ (buy) a new dress. Do you like it?

8. He ___ (not / feel) well recently.

9. How many times ___ you ___ (see) that film?

10. I ___ (always / believe) that honesty is the best policy.

Упражнение 2: Past Perfect Simple vs. Continuous

1. He was angry. He ___ (argue) with his wife all morning.

2. By the time I arrived, the meeting ___ (already / start).

3. They ___ (drive) for about an hour when the car broke down.

4. She ___ (never / fly) before, so she was nervous.

5. The ground was wet. It ___ (rain) for hours.

6. He ___ (finish) the report by the time his boss asked for it.

7. Her hands were covered in flour because she ___ (bake) bread.

8. I didn't recognize her because she ___ (change) a lot.

9. They were out of breath because they ___ (run) to catch the bus.

10. How long ___ you ___ (wait) before you realized you were in the wrong place?

Упражнение 3: Future Perfect Simple vs. Continuous

1. By next Monday, I ___ (finish) this project.

2. By the end of this year, she ___ (live) in London for 10 years.

3. By the time you arrive, we ___ (eat) all the food.

4. In June, he ___ (work) at this company for five years.

5. I hope I ___ (save) enough money to buy a car by next summer.

6. By the time the flight lands, we ___ (fly) for 8 hours straight.

7. They ___ (not / finish) painting the house by Friday.

8. Next month, I ___ (learn) Chinese for two years.

9. By the time you get here, the movie ___.

10. How long ___ you ___ (wait) by 6 PM?

Упражнение 4: Choosing the Right Perfect Tense (Mixed)

Выберите правильное время: Present, Past или Future Perfect.

1. I can't come out tonight. I ___ (not finish) my essay yet.

2. By the time I move next month, I ___ (live) here for exactly 10 years.

3. She ___ (work) there for five years before she quit last year.

4. This is the first time I ___ (ever / eat) squid.

5. He realized he ___ (see) the film before, so he turned off the TV.

6. Don't call me after 10 PM. I ___ (go) to bed by then.

7. I'm sorry, I ___ (not / hear) from him for weeks. I hope he's okay.

8. They weren't at home when I called. They ___ (go) out.

9. By the end of our trip, we ___ (visit) five countries.

10. He ___ (just / arrive) home from a long journey. He looks very tired.

Упражнение 5: Sentence Transformation

Выберите предложение с Perfect Tense, которое лучше всего описывает ситуацию.

1. Situation: I started watching a film at 7 PM. It is now 8 PM and I am still watching it.

2. Situation: First, he finished his work. Then, he went home.

3. Situation: My flight is at 8 PM. The check-in will close at 7 PM.

4. Situation: I am tired. The reason is that I was painting my room all morning.

5. Situation: I started waiting at 2 PM. At 4 PM, my friend finally arrived.

6. Situation: This is her third visit to Japan.

7. Situation: I will start my new job on June 1st. Next year, on June 1st, will be my one-year anniversary.

8. Situation: He lost his keys. As a result, he couldn't get into his apartment.

9. Situation: She will finish her presentation at 4 PM. You can call her at 4:15 PM.

10. Situation: I started running at 8 AM. It is now 8:30 AM. I am still running.
