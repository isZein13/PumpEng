Nominalization (Номинализация)

Упражнения на преобразование глаголов и прилагательных в существительные для формального стиля.

Номинализация — это процесс создания существительного из глагола или прилагательного. Это ключевой элемент формального и академического английского, так как позволяет выражать идеи более абстрактно и объективно.

  • Verb → Noun:
    • The company decided to expand. → The company's decision was to expand.
    • They analyzed the data. → Their analysis of the data was thorough.
  • Adjective → Noun:
    • The task was difficult. → The difficulty of the task was obvious.
    • It is important to be patient. → The importance of patience cannot be overstated.

Упражнение 1: Verb to Noun Transformation

Выберите правильное существительное, образованное от глагола в скобках.

1. The police launched a full ___ into the case. (investigate)

2. His ___ of the situation was completely wrong. (assess)

3. The ___ of a new manager improved the team's morale. (arrive)

4. Her ___ to help was greatly appreciated. (refuse)

5. We need a clear ___ of the project goals. (define)

6. The rapid ___ of the city has caused many problems. (grow)

7. Her ___ from the company was a surprise to everyone. (depart)

8. There has been a significant ___ in sales this quarter. (reduce)

9. His ___ of the offer was unexpected. (reject)

10. The ___ of the new software will begin next month. (develop)

2. The ___ of this information is not guaranteed. (accurate)

3. Her ___ to learn new things is remarkable. (able)

4. I don't understand the ___ of this rule. (important)

5. The ___ of the problem was underestimated. (complex)

6. ___ is the key to a good friendship. (Honest)

7. We need to improve the ___ of our products. (reliable)

8. The ___ between the two pictures is obvious. (similar)

9. His main ___ is that he never gives up. (strong)

10. The ___ of the local people was heartwarming. (kind)
