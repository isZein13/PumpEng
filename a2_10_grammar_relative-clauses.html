Relative Clauses (Относительные предложения)

Упражнения на использование 'who', 'which', 'that', 'whose' и 'where' для соединения предложений.

Relative Clauses – это придаточные предложения, которые дают дополнительную информацию о существительном из главного предложения. Они вводятся относительными местоимениями.

  • who / that – для людей: The man who/that lives next door is a doctor.
  • which / that – для предметов и животных: This is the book which/that I bought.
  • whose – для принадлежности (чей): That's the woman whose dog is always barking.
  • where – для мест: This is the house where I was born.

Упражнение 1: 'who' для людей

1. The woman ___ works at the bank is very friendly.

2. I know a man ___ can speak six languages.

3. The police are looking for the person ___ broke the window.

4. The student ___ sits next to me is from China.

5. I don't like people ___ are always late.

6. The girl ___ you met yesterday is my cousin.

7. A doctor is someone ___ helps sick people.

8. He's the actor ___ was in "Titanic".

9. The children ___ shouted in the street are my neighbours.

10. I want to talk to the manager ___ is in charge.

Упражнение 2: 'which' и 'that' для предметов

1. This is the book ___ I told you about.

2. The car ___ is parked outside is mine.

3. I don't like films ___ have unhappy endings.

4. Where is the cheese ___ was in the fridge?

5. The keys ___ were on the table are gone.

6. The dog ___ is chasing the cat is ours.

7. I lost the watch ___ you gave me.

8. The phone ___ I bought last week is already broken.

9. Is this the bus ___ goes to the city center?

10. The company ___ he works for is very big.

Упражнение 3: 'whose' для принадлежности

1. That's the man ___ car was stolen.

2. I have a friend ___ brother is a famous actor.

3. The girl ___ phone is ringing should answer it.

4. We saw a house ___ roof was made of glass.

5. This is the student ___ homework was the best.

6. The dog ___ owner is on holiday is staying with us.

7. She is married to a man ___ father is a millionaire.

8. Is that the boy ___ bicycle was stolen?

9. I found a cat ___ leg was broken.

10. The author ___ books are very popular will visit our city.

Упражнение 4: 'where' для мест

1. This is the house ___ I grew up.

2. A cafe is a place ___ you can drink coffee.

3. I remember the park ___ we first met.

4. Is that the shop ___ you bought your shoes?

5. The hotel ___ we stayed was very nice.

6. I want to visit the city ___ my parents were born.

7. A library is a place ___ you can read books.

8. That's the restaurant ___ we had dinner last night.

9. We found a small village ___ nobody lived.

10. The room ___ we have our meetings is on the top floor.

Упражнение 5: Смешанная практика

Выберите наиболее подходящее слово: who, which, that, whose, where.

1. That's the girl ___ won the competition.

2. I read a book ___ was very interesting.

3. This is the hospital ___ I was born.

4. I met a woman ___ husband works in my company.

5. What's the name of the restaurant ___ we ate last week?

6. The food ___ she cooked was delicious.

7. An architect is a person ___ designs buildings.

8. He lives in a house ___ garden is beautiful.

9. This is the town ___ my grandfather grew up.

10. The people ___ flight was cancelled were very angry.
