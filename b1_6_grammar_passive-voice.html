Passive Voice (Simple & Continuous Tenses)

Упражнения на пассивный залог для действий в прошлом, настоящем и будущем.

На уровне B1 мы расширяем использование Пассивного залога, добавляя длительные времена. Пассивный залог используется, когда фокус смещается с исполнителя действия на объект, над которым это действие совершается.

  • Present Simple Passive: is/are + V3 (The office is cleaned every day.)
  • Past Simple Passive: was/were + V3 (This book was written in 1950.)
  • Future Simple Passive: will be + V3 (The report will be finished tomorrow.)
  • Present Continuous Passive: is/are + being + V3 (The road is being repaired now.)
  • Past Continuous Passive: was/were + being + V3 (The bridge was being built when I was there.)

Упражнение 1: Simple Tenses Passive (Review)

1. "Romeo and Juliet" ___ (write) by Shakespeare.

2. The results of the exam ___ (announce) next week.

3. German ___ (speak) in Austria and Switzerland.

4. My car ___ (repair) yesterday.

5. The new bridge ___ (open) to the public tomorrow.

6. The gallery ___ (visit) by thousands of people every year.

7. This photo ___ (take) when I was a child.

8. The letters ___ (not / deliver) yet, they will be delivered tomorrow.

9. America ___ (discover) in 1492.

10. The meeting ___ (cancel) if the manager is ill tomorrow.

Упражнение 2: Continuous Tenses Passive

1. Please be quiet. A test ___ (take) in the next room.

2. When I arrived, the dinner ___ (serve).

3. Don't enter the room. The floor ___ (clean) right now.

4. At 5 PM yesterday, the final touches ___ (put) on the project.

5. Our house can't be sold at the moment because it ___. (redecorate)

6. The road was closed because a new bridge ___. (build)

7. A new TV series ___ (film) in our street at the moment.

8. This room can't be used. It ___ for a meeting. (prepare)

9. While the photos ___, we waited outside. (develop)

10. An important experiment ___ (conduct) in the lab now.

Упражнение 3: Future Simple & Modal Passive

1. This problem must ___ (solve) immediately.

2. The report ___ (finish) by tomorrow morning.

3. Your order ___ (deliver) within three business days.

4. This task can ___ (do) by anyone on the team.

5. The winners ___ (choose) next week.

6. The museum might ___ (close) for renovations.

7. All the old files should ___ (archive).

8. This car cannot ___ (park) here.

9. The new rules ___ (introduce) at the next meeting.

10. The package should ___ (send) as soon as possible.

Упражнение 4: Active or Passive? (Mixed Tenses)

1. While the presentation ___, the audience asked many questions.

2. Somebody ___ my bike last night!

3. Look at the new stadium! It ___ very quickly.

4. This problem ___ tomorrow, I promise.

5. The police ___ three suspects so far.

6. This room ___ for a special meeting, so we can't use it.

7. When ___ the first car ___?

8. Our company ___ over 500 people.

9. These computers ___ in this factory.

10. While my car ___, I was waiting in the cafe.

Упражнение 5: Sentence Transformation (Active to Passive)

Выберите правильное пассивное предложение, которое соответствует активному.

1. Active: They are building a new hospital.

2. Active: Somebody will clean the room later.

3. Active: The police were questioning him for an hour.

4. Active: Someone has stolen my laptop!

5. Active: People must not leave bicycles in the hallway.

6. Active: They make these shoes in Italy.

7. Active: The company will launch the new product next year.

8. Active: Someone was serving dinner when we arrived.

9. Active: You can't use your phone in the exam.
