Present Simple (Настоящее простое время)

Упражнения для отработки регулярных действий, привычек и фактов.

Present Simple используется для описания действий, которые происходят регулярно, обычно, всегда (привычки, рутина), а также для общеизвестных фактов и истин.
Утверждения: I/You/We/They + V1 (play). He/She/It + V1s (plays).
Отрицания: I/You/We/They + don't + V1 (don't play). He/She/It + doesn't + V1 (doesn't play).
Вопросы: Do + I/you/we/they + V1? (Do you play?). Does + he/she/it + V1? (Does she play?).

Упражнение 1: Утверждения (I, You, We, They)

1. I ___ (to read) books in the evening.

2. We ___ (to play) football on Saturdays.

3. You ___ (to speak) English very well.

4. They ___ (to live) in a big house.

5. My friends ___ (to like) pizza.

6. I ___ (to get up) at 7 o'clock every morning.

7. You ___ (to drink) coffee for breakfast.

8. We ___ (to go) to the cinema on Fridays.

9. They ___ (to study) English at school.

10. I ___ (to have) two brothers.

Упражнение 2: Утверждения (He, She, It)

1. My father ___ (to work) in a bank.

2. She ___ (to teach) music at our school.

3. The cat ___ (to sleep) on the sofa.

4. He ___ (to go) to the gym three times a week.

5. My mother ___ (to cook) delicious dinners.

6. It usually ___ (to rain) a lot in autumn.

7. My friend ___ (to speak) Spanish.

8. The bus ___ (to arrive) at 8:30 AM.

9. She ___ (to have) a new car.

10. The Earth ___ (to go) around the Sun.

Упражнение 3: Отрицания (don't / doesn't)

1. I ___ (not / like) spicy food.

2. He ___ (not / play) the piano.

3. We ___ (not / go) to the beach in winter.

4. She ___ (not / work) on Sundays.

5. You ___ (not / study) German.

6. The dog ___ (not / eat) vegetables.

7. My parents ___ (not / live) in this city.

8. It ___ (not / snow) here in summer.

9. I ___ (not / have) a car.

10. He ___ (not / watch) horror films.

Упражнение 4: Вопросы (Do / Does)

1. ___ you like apples?

2. ___ your friend play the guitar?

3. ___ they live near here?

4. ___ it often snow in your city?

5. ___ we have a test today?

6. ___ your cat drink milk?

7. ___ I know you?

8. ___ he speak English?

9. ___ your parents like travelling?

10. ___ she go to bed early?

Упражнение 5: Факты и рутина

1. Water ___ (to boil) at 100 degrees Celsius.

2. I usually ___ (to have) breakfast at 8 AM.

3. The Earth ___ (to revolve) around the Sun.

4. Cats ___ (to like) milk.

5. My school ___ (to start) at 9 o'clock.

6. He often ___ (to visit) his grandparents.

7. We always ___ (to clean) our room on Saturdays.

8. The moon ___ (to shine) at night.

9. My dad ___ (to drive) to work.

10. Birds ___ (to fly).
