Читайте тексты разной сложности и отвечайте на вопросы, чтобы улучшить свои навыки чтения и понимания английского языка.

Уровень A1 (Beginner)

Текст 1: My Cat

This is my cat. His name is Tom. Tom is a big cat. He is black and white. Tom likes to sleep. He sleeps on my bed. He also likes to play with a small ball. I love my cat Tom.

Вопросы к тексту "My Cat":

1. What is the cat's name?

2. What color is Tom?

3. Where does Tom like to sleep?

Уровень B2 (Upper-Intermediate)

Текст 1: The Tetris Effect

Tetris effect is a syndrome named after the video game Tetris. It occurs when one devotes an excessive amount of time to an activity or game, which begins to overtake the person's thoughts and dreams. At its release in 1984, the game of Tetris took the world by storm and people began to devote massive amounts of time to it. Players began to see the game's abstract shapes in everyday life, and visualized how pieces of furniture and other objects would fit together to form perfect rows, for example. They even saw falling blocks in their dreams. Though Tetris’ heyday is long behind us, the Tetris effect still lives on. It can emerge as a consequence of ordinary hobbies, tasks, or playing other games. It is also prevalent in computer programmers and mathematicians who often see lines of code and complex equations as they try to fall asleep at night.

Вопросы к тексту "The Tetris Effect":

1. According to the passage, the Tetris effect is a syndrome that _____.

2. As pointed out in the passage, the game of Tetris _____.

3. The purpose of this passage is to _____.
