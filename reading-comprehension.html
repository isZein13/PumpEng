Упражнения на Понимание Текста

Читайте тексты разной сложности и отвечайте на вопросы, чтобы улучшить свои навыки чтения и понимания английского языка.

Уровень A1 (Beginner)

Текст 1: My Pet Rabbit

I have a pet. It is a rabbit. My rabbit's name is Fluffy. Fluffy is white. She has long ears and a small pink nose. Fluffy likes carrots and green leaves. She lives in a hutch in my garden. I play with Fluffy every day. She is very soft and cute.

Вопросы к тексту "My Pet Rabbit":

1. What kind of pet is Fluffy?

2. What color is Fluffy?

3. What does Fluffy like to eat?

Уровень A2 (Elementary)

Текст 1: A Day at the Beach

Yesterday, my family and I went to the beach. The weather was sunny and warm. We took a big umbrella and some towels. My brother, Sam, and I built a large sandcastle near the water. Later, we swam in the sea. The water was a little cold, but it was fun. For lunch, we had sandwiches and fruit. In the afternoon, we played volleyball. I was tired but very happy when we went home.

Вопросы к тексту "A Day at the Beach":

1. What was the weather like?

2. What did the narrator and Sam build?

3. What did they have for lunch?

Уровень B1 (Intermediate)

Текст 1: My First Job Interview

[ЗАПОЛНИТЕ ТЕКСТ B1 ЗДЕСЬ: История о первом собеседовании. Должен содержать Past Simple, возможно, немного Present Perfect для описания опыта. Лексика о чувствах, подготовке, самом процессе интервью и результате. Примерно 120-150 слов.]

Example: I remember my first job interview clearly. I had just graduated from university and was looking for a position as a junior marketing assistant. I spent days preparing, researching the company, and practicing common interview questions. On the day, I was incredibly nervous, but I tried to stay calm. The interviewer asked me about my studies, my strengths, and why I wanted to work for their company. I think I answered most questions well, though I stumbled on one or two. A week later, they called to offer me the job! I was so excited.

Вопросы к тексту "My First Job Interview":

1. What position was the narrator applying for?

2. How did the narrator feel on the day of the interview?

3. What was the result of the interview?

Уровень B2 (Upper-Intermediate)

Текст 1: The Importance of Urban Green Spaces

[ЗАПОЛНИТЕ ТЕКСТ B2 ЗДЕСЬ: Обсуждение важности зеленых зон в городах. Может включать Conditionals, Passive Voice, более сложную лексику. Примерно 180-220 слов.]

Example: Urban green spaces, such as parks, public gardens, and even small community plots, play a crucial role in the well-being of city dwellers and the overall health of an urban environment. Beyond their aesthetic appeal, these areas provide essential ecosystem services. They help to mitigate the urban heat island effect, improve air quality by absorbing pollutants, and reduce stormwater runoff. Furthermore, access to green spaces has been consistently linked to improved mental and physical health. Studies have shown that spending time in nature can reduce stress, boost mood, and encourage physical activity. As cities continue to grow, the anning and preservation of these green oases are becoming increasingly vital for creating sustainable and livable urban futures.

Вопросы к тексту "The Importance of Urban Green Spaces":

1. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a benefit of urban green spaces?

2. The "urban heat island effect" refers to _____.

3. According to the passage, what is essential for sustainable urban futures?

Уровень C1 (Advanced)

Текст 1: The Nuances of Cross-Cultural Communication

[ЗАПОЛНИТЕ ТЕКСТ C1 ЗДЕСЬ: Сложный текст о межкультурной коммуникации, возможно, с идиомами, сложными грамматическими конструкциями, абстрактными понятиями. Примерно 250-300 слов.]

Example: Effective cross-cultural communication extends far beyond mere linguistic proficiency; it necessitates a profound understanding of implicit cultural norms, varying communication styles, and non-verbal cues that can significantly differ across societies. What might be considered direct and efficient in one culture could be perceived as abrupt or even rude in another. High-context cultures, for instance, rely heavily on shared experiences and unspoken understandings, where meaning is often embedded in the context rather than explicitly stated. Conversely, low-context cultures tend to favor explicit, direct communication. Navigating these subtleties requires not only cultural sensitivity and empathy but also a willingness to adapt one's own communication approach to foster mutual understanding and avoid potential misunderstandings that can hinder international collaborations and personal relationships.

Вопросы к тексту "The Nuances of Cross-Cultural Communication":

1. According to the passage, what is crucial for effective cross-cultural communication beyond language skills?

2. What characterizes high-context cultures, as mentioned in the text?

3. What can hinder international collaborations, according to the passage?
