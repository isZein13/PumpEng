Упражнения на Понимание Текста

Читайте тексты разной сложности и отвечайте на вопросы, чтобы улучшить свои навыки чтения и понимания английского языка.

Уровень A1 (Beginner)

Текст 1: My School Day

My name is Anna. I am a student. I go to school every day from Monday to Friday. My school is big. I have many friends at school. My favorite subject is English. I like to read books and learn new words. After school, I do my homework. Then I play with my little dog, Lucky. Lucky is very funny.

Вопросы к тексту "My School Day":

1. What is Anna's favorite subject?

2. When does Anna go to school?

3. What does Anna do after school before playing with her dog?

Уровень A2 (Elementary)

Текст 1: A Surprise Birthday Party

Last Saturday was my friend Leo's birthday. We decided to have a surprise party for him. His sister, Mia, helped us. We bought a big chocolate cake and some colorful balloons. We invited all his close friends. When Leo came home from his football practice, everyone shouted "Surprise!" He was very surprised and happy. We played games, ate cake, and listened to music. Leo said it was his best birthday ever.

Вопросы к тексту "A Surprise Birthday Party":

1. Who helped to organize the party?

2. What did they buy for the party?

3. How did Leo feel when he saw the surprise?

Уровень B1 (Intermediate)

Текст 1: Choosing a Career Path

Choosing a career path is one of the most significant decisions a young person makes. It's often influenced by various factors such as personal interests, skills, parental expectations, and perceived job market demands. Some people know exactly what they want to do from an early age, while others might explore several options before settling on a profession. It's important to research different careers, understand the required qualifications, and consider the long-term prospects. Talking to professionals in fields of interest can also provide valuable insights. Ultimately, finding a career that is both fulfilling and provides a stable income is a common goal, though the definition of "fulfilling" can vary greatly from person to person.

Вопросы к тексту "Choosing a Career Path":

1. Which of these is NOT mentioned as a factor influencing career choice?

2. What can provide valuable insights when choosing a career, according to the text?

3. What is described as a common goal in finding a career?

Уровень B2 (Upper-Intermediate)

Текст 1: The Rise of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming various sectors, from healthcare and finance to entertainment and transportation. Its ability to process vast amounts of data and perform complex tasks with increasing accuracy has led to significant advancements. However, the proliferation of AI also raises profound ethical questions and societal concerns. Issues such as job displacement due to automation, algorithmic bias, and the potential for misuse in surveillance or autonomous weaponry demand careful consideration and robust regulatory frameworks. As AI continues to evolve, a multidisciplinary approach involving technologists, policymakers, ethicists, and the public will be crucial to harnessing its benefits while mitigating its risks.

Вопросы к тексту "The Rise of Artificial Intelligence":

1. According to the passage, what is one of the capabilities of AI?

2. Which of the following is mentioned as a societal concern regarding AI?

3. What does the passage suggest is crucial as AI continues to evolve?

Уровень C1 (Advanced)

Текст 1: The Subjectivity of Historical Narratives

The interpretation of historical events is seldom a monolithic endeavor; rather, it is a complex tapestry woven from diverse perspectives, biases, and the inherent limitations of available evidence. Historians, despite striving for objectivity, are inevitably influenced by their own cultural, social, and temporal contexts. Consequently, historical narratives are not static truths etched in stone but are continuously re-evaluated and reinterpreted as new evidence emerges or as societal values shift. The very act of selecting which events to highlight and which to omit can profoundly shape the resultant narrative. This inherent subjectivity underscores the importance of engaging with multiple historical accounts and critically analyzing the methodologies and motivations fatores. It is a reminder that history is as much about the present's dialogue with the past as it is about the past itself.
