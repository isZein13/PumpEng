Relative Clauses

Упражнения на определительные и неопределительные придаточные предложения.

На уровне B1 мы учимся различать два типа относительных предложений:

  • Defining Relative Clauses (Определительные): Дают важную информацию, без которой непонятно, о ком или о чем идет речь. Не выделяются запятыми. Можно использовать who, which, that, whose, where.
    • The woman who lives next door is a doctor. (Без этой части мы не знаем, о какой женщине речь).
  • Non-defining Relative Clauses (Неопределительные): Дают дополнительную, не обязательную информацию. Всегда выделяются запятыми. Можно использовать who, which, whose, where, но нельзя использовать that.
    • My brother, who lives in London, is a lawyer. (Мы и так знаем, о ком речь - о моем брате).

Упражнение 1: Defining Clauses (Review)

Выберите правильное местоимение. Запятые не нужны.

1. This is the phone ___ I bought yesterday.

2. The woman ___ called you works in my office.

3. I don't like stories ___ have sad endings.

4. What's the name of the man ___ car you borrowed?

5. The restaurant ___ we had dinner was very good.

6. The police arrested the man ___ robbed the bank.

7. I can't find the CD ___ I bought this morning.

8. This is the town ___ I spent my childhood.

9. Do you know the girl ___ is talking to Tom?

10. She's the artist ___ paintings are incredibly expensive.

Упражнение 2: Non-defining Clauses

Выберите правильное местоимение. Помните, что 'that' не используется в этих предложениях.

1. My brother, ___ lives in Australia, is a doctor.

2. The Eiffel Tower, ___ is in Paris, is a famous landmark.

3. Sarah, ___ I've known for years, is a very reliable person.

4. My car, ___ I bought last year, is very economical.

5. Mr. Smith, ___ is my neighbour, works as a teacher.

6. I'm reading a book by Jane Austen, ___ is my favorite author.

7. We went to the new Italian restaurant, ___ was a nice surprise.

8. My phone, ___ is only a year old, has stopped working.

9. The Amazon River, ___ flows through Brazil, is the largest in the world.

10. I spoke to John, ___ told me you were away.

Упражнение 3: 'whose' и 'where'

Выберите 'whose' для принадлежности или 'where' для места.

1. I visited the village ___ my grandparents were born.

2. That's the politician ___ opinions are very controversial.

3. This is the office ___ I work.

4. I have a neighbour ___ dog barks all night.

5. We went to Rome, ___ we saw the Colosseum.

6. The man ___ wallet I found thanked me.

7. A hotel is a place ___ people stay on holiday.

8. That's the girl ___ ambition is to be a pilot.

9. Do you remember the cafe ___ we used to meet?

10. The teacher, ___ name I can't remember, was very kind.

Упражнение 4: Defining или Non-defining? (Пунктуация)

Решите, является ли информация важной (defining) или дополнительной (non-defining), и выберите правильный вариант с запятыми или без.

1. My brother who lives in Canada is older than me. (I have more than one brother)

2. My brother who lives in Canada is older than me. (I have only one brother)

3. The book that I'm reading is very interesting. (Specifies which book)

4. I went to see the film "Dune" which was fantastic. (Gives extra information about the specific film)

5. The people who I work with are very friendly. (Specifies which people)

6. We had lunch at "The Italian Kitchen" which is a new restaurant. (Extra information)

7. This is the hotel where we stayed last year. (Essential information)

8. Mrs. Smith whose son is a famous singer lives next door. (Extra information about Mrs. Smith)

9. He gave me a letter which was in a blue envelope. (Essential information to identify the letter)

10. London which is the capital of the UK has a population of over 8 million. (Extra information about London)

Упражнение 5: Объединение предложений

1. A girl was injured in the accident. She is now in the hospital.

2. I went to a restaurant. It was very expensive.

3. My sister works in a bank. Her name is Anna.

4. I recently went back to my hometown. I was born there.

5. A man answered the phone. He told me you were away.

6. This is a story about a girl. Her parents are very rich.

7. I bought a new computer. It has a very fast processor.

8. We stayed at the Park Hotel. A friend of ours had recommended it.

9. I was looking for a book this morning. I've found it now.

10. My friend is a famous architect. His name is Peter.
