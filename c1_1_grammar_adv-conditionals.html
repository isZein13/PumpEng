Advanced Conditionals

Упражнения на инверсию и альтернативные союзы в условных предложениях.

На уровне C1 мы осваиваем более сложные и формальные способы выражения условий.

  • Inversion in Conditionals (Инверсия): Замена `if` путем изменения порядка слов.
    • If I were... → Were I...
    • If I had known... → Had I known...
    • If you should need... → Should you need...
  • Alternatives to `if` (Альтернативы `if`):
    • unless (если не): I won't go unless you come with me.
    • provided/providing that (при условии, что): You can borrow my car, provided that you drive carefully.
    • as long as (пока; при условии, что): You can stay here as long as you keep quiet.
    • in case (на случай, если): Take an umbrella in case it rains. (Действие-предосторожность)

Упражнение 1: Review of Mixed Conditionals

1. If he ___ (take) my advice back then, he ___ (be) a rich man now.

2. If I ___ (not be) so afraid of flying, I ___ (go) with you to Brazil last month.

3. We ___ (not be) in this difficult situation now if you ___ (listen) to me.

4. If she ___ (be) more ambitious, she ___ (apply) for that promotion last year.

5. If I ___ (eat) breakfast this morning, I ___ (not be) so hungry now.

6. He ___ (pass) the exam if he weren't so lazy.

7. If I didn't have to work tomorrow, I ___ (stay) out late with you last night.

8. She would be a much happier person if she ___ (not marry) him all those years ago.

9. If you ___ (check) the map, we wouldn't be lost now.

10. I would have called you yesterday if I ___ your number. (but I don't have it in general)

Упражнение 2: Inversion in Conditionals

1. ___ I known the truth, I would have acted differently.

2. ___ you to change your mind, please let me know.

3. ___ I you, I would reconsider that offer.

4. ___ seen the warning signs, they would not have entered the building.

5. ___ a millionaire, he would donate a large sum to charity.

6. ___ need any further assistance, do not hesitate to contact us.

7. ___ not for his help, we would have failed.

8. ___ to win the lottery, he would travel the world.

9. ___ listened to the weather forecast, we would have taken an umbrella.

10. ___ more responsible, he would have been promoted.

Упражнение 3: Alternatives to 'if'

1. I won't help you ___ you promise to be more careful.

2. You can borrow the car, ___ you don't drive too fast.

3. I'll be at my desk until 6 PM ___ you need me.

4. You will fail the exam ___ you start studying immediately.

5. You can join the club ___ you are over 18.

6. The bank will lend you the money ___ you have a good credit score.

7. ___ you apologize, she will never speak to you again.

8. Take your phone with you ___ you need to call me.

9. I don't mind what you do, ___ it doesn't disturb me.

10. We'll miss the beginning of the movie ___ we leave now.

Упражнение 4: 'in case' vs. 'if'

Выберите правильный союз: 'if' (условие) или 'in case' (предосторожность).

1. I'll buy some extra food ___ more guests arrive.

2. ___ more guests arrive, we won't have enough chairs.

3. You should write down her number ___ you forget it.

4. I'll lend you my umbrella, but only ___ you promise to bring it back.

5. I always take a book with me ___ I have to wait.

6. Let's check the flight time again ___ it has changed.

7. I'll only go to the party ___ you go too.

8. He charged his phone fully ___ he couldn't find a socket later.

9. ___ I see him, I will give him your message.

10. I'll make an extra sandwich ___ you get hungry later.
