Reported Speech (Statements, Questions, Commands)

Комплексная практика косвенной речи: передача утверждений, вопросов и команд.

На уровне B1 мы объединяем и систематизируем все типы косвенной речи. Основное правило — "шаг назад во времени" и изменение порядка слов в вопросах.

  • Statements (Утверждения): "I am tired." → He said he was tired. (Используем `say` или `tell`).
  • Questions (Вопросы):
    • Yes/No: "Are you busy?" → She asked if I was busy.
    • Wh-: "Where do you live?" → He asked where I lived. (Порядок слов прямой, `do/does/did` исчезают).
  • Commands (Команды): "Close the door." → He told me to close the door. (Используется инфинитив `to + V`).

2. "We have finished our work." → They said they ___ their work.

3. "She will call you tomorrow." → He told me she ___ me the next day.

4. "I can't swim." → She said she ___.

5. "He is a doctor." → I was told he ___ a doctor.

6. "I'm going to the cinema." → She said she ___ to the cinema.

7. "They were playing football." → He said they ___.

8. "I may be late." → She said she ___ be late.

9. "I haven't seen that film." → He said he ___ that film.

10. "We lived here for three years." → They said they ___ there for three years.

Упражнение 2: Questions

2. "Where is the station?" → He asked where the station ___.

3. "Do you speak English?" → She asked me if I ___ English.

4. "What did you do?" → He asked me what I ___.

5. "Have you finished?" → My boss asked if I ___.

6. "When can we meet?" → She asked when we ___.

7. "Will you help me?" → He asked if I ___ help him.

8. "Why are you late?" → The teacher asked me why I ___ late.

9. "Does she know about the plan?" → He wanted to know if she ___ about the plan.

3. Please ___ me the story again.

4. I want to ___ something important.

5. What did he ___ to you?

6. The doctor ___ him to rest for a week.

7. She didn't ___ goodbye when she left.

8. Can you ___ me the difference between these two words?

9. He ___ he had never been to Italy.
