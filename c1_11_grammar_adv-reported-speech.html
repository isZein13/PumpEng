Advanced Reported Speech

Упражнения на передачу сложных утверждений, вопросов и предложений с reporting verbs.

На уровне C1 мы расширяем арсенал глаголов для передачи косвенной речи (reporting verbs), которые требуют после себя герундий или инфинитив, и рассматриваем сложные случаи сдвига модальных глаголов.

  • Reporting Verb + Gerund: `admit`, `deny`, `suggest`. "Let's go to the cinema." → He suggested going to the cinema.
  • Reporting Verb + Infinitive: `promise`, `offer`, `refuse`, `agree`. "I'll help you." → He promised to help me.
  • Reporting Verb + Object + Infinitive: `advise`, `warn`, `remind`. "You should see a doctor." → He advised me to see a doctor.
  • Reporting Modals:
    • `must` (obligation) → `had to`. "I must finish this." → He said he had to finish it.
    • `must` (deduction), `might`, `should`, `ought to` → обычно не меняются. "He must be tired." → She thought he must be tired.

Упражнение 1: Reporting Verbs + Gerund/Infinitive

1. "I didn't break the window," he said. → He denied ___ the window.

2. "I will definitely pay you back," she said. → She promised ___ me back.

3. "Why don't we go to the beach?" he said. → He suggested ___ to the beach.

4. "Don't touch the live wire!" the electrician said. → The electrician warned us ___.

5. "It was you who told the secret!" she said to him. → She accused him of ___ the secret.

6. "I'm sorry I was late," he said. → He apologized for ___.

7. "You should apply for the job," she told him. → She encouraged him ___.

8. "Go on, take the last piece of cake," she said. → She insisted on me ___ the last piece of cake.

9. "I won't help you," he said. → He refused ___.

10. "Well done! You passed the exam," the teacher said. → The teacher congratulated me on ___.

Упражнение 2: Reporting with Modal Verbs

1. "You must finish the report by Friday." → She said I ___ finish the report by Friday.

2. "He must be very rich." → She thought that he ___ be very rich.

3. "You should have been more careful." → He told me I ___ more careful.

4. "I might not come to the party." → He said he ___ to the party.

5. "She must have known the secret." → He was sure that she ___ the secret.

6. "You needn't worry." → She told me I ___ worry.

7. "He can't have said that!" → She was convinced that he ___ that.

8. "I had to work late last night." → He explained that he ___ late the night before.

9. "You ought to be more careful." → He told her she ___ more careful.

10. "She may have forgotten the appointment." → He thought she ___ the appointment.

Упражнение 3: Reporting with Subjunctive or 'should'

1. "I suggest that he ___ a doctor."

2. The law demands that everyone ___ a seatbelt.

3. I recommended that she ___ for the job.

4. He insisted that the contract ___ signed immediately.

5. It is essential that all students ___ the exam.

6. The doctor recommended that he ___ more rest.

7. The rules require that each participant ___ their own equipment.

8. She requested that the information ___ kept confidential.

9. He urged that the government ___ immediate action.

10. It is vital that you ___ on time for the interview.

Упражнение 4: Reporting Conditionals

Выберите правильный вариант косвенной речи для условных предложений.

1. "If I have time, I will call you." → He said that if he ___ time, he ___ call me.

2. "If I were you, I would apologize." → She told him that if she ___ him, she ___ apologize.

3. "If I had known, I would have helped." → He said that if he ___, he ___.

4. "What will you do if you fail?" → She asked me what I ___ if I ___.

5. "If it rains, we won't go out." → They said that if it ___, they ___.

6. "If I had a car, I would drive you." → He said that if he ___ a car, he ___ me.

7. "If I had studied, I wouldn't have failed." → She said that if she ___, she ___.

8. "What would you do if you were me?" → He asked me what I ___ if I ___ him.

9. "We will be late if the traffic is bad." → I warned them that they ___ late if the traffic ___ bad.

10. "If you had asked, I would have told you." → She said that if I ___, she ___.

Упражнение 5: Rephrasing & Nuance

Выберите наиболее точный и стилистически верный вариант косвенной речи.

1. "I'm absolutely sure he took the money," she said.

2. "Don't forget to submit your application," the secretary said.

3. "I wish I hadn't spoken so rudely," he said.

4. "What if we postpone the meeting until Friday?" she said.

5. "If you don't give me the money, I'll tell everyone your secret," he said to her.

6. "I didn't do it! I swear!" the boy said.

7. "You should definitely try this new restaurant," my friend said.

8. "I'm sorry, I forgot your name," he said.

9. "You must tell me the truth," she said.

10. "If I were you, I'd take the job," he said.
