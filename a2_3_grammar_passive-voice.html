Passive Voice (Пассивный залог)

Упражнения на ситуации, когда действие совершается НАД предметом или человеком.

Пассивный (страдательный) залог (Passive Voice) используется, когда нам важнее объект, над которым совершается действие, а не тот, кто его совершает. Исполнитель действия может быть упомянут с предлогом by или не упомянут вовсе.

  • Present Simple Passive: am/is/are + V3 (третья форма глагола)
    • Active: Someone cleans this room every day.
    • Passive: This room is cleaned every day.
  • Past Simple Passive: was/were + V3 (третья форма глагола)
    • Active: Shakespeare wrote "Hamlet".
    • Passive: "Hamlet" was written by Shakespeare.

Упражнение 1: Основы (Present Simple Passive)

1. English ___ (speak) all over the world.

2. This room ___ (clean) every day.

3. These cars ___ (make) in Japan.

4. I ___ (pay) on the last day of the month.

5. The windows ___ (wash) twice a year.

6. Oranges ___ (import) into our country.

7. How ___ this word ___ (pronounce)?

8. Cheese ___ (make) from milk.

9. Many accidents ___ (cause) by careless driving.

10. The office ___ (lock) at 7 PM every evening.

Упражнение 2: Основы (Past Simple Passive)

1. My car ___ (steal) last night.

2. This bridge ___ (build) in 1965.

3. These houses ___ (damage) in the storm.

4. The suspect ___ (arrest) by the police yesterday.

5. We ___ (not / invite) to the party.

6. The first pyramids ___ (construct) in Egypt.

7. When ___ the telephone ___ (invent)?

8. I ___ (give) this watch by my grandfather.

9. The letters ___ (send) a week ago.

10. The problem ___ (solve) by the engineers.

Упражнение 3: Трансформация (Актив → Пассив)

Преобразуйте активное предложение в пассивное, выбрав правильный вариант.

1. Active: Somebody cleans the office every day. → Passive: The office ___ every day.

2. Active: They built these houses in 2001. → Passive: These houses ___ in 2001.

3. Active: Picasso painted "Guernica". → Passive: "Guernica" ___ by Picasso.

4. Active: People speak Portuguese in Brazil. → Passive: Portuguese ___ in Brazil.

5. Active: My grandmother made this cake. → Passive: This cake ___ by my grandmother.

6. Active: Someone stole my wallet. → Passive: My wallet ___.

7. Active: They don't deliver mail on Sundays. → Passive: Mail ___ on Sundays.

8. Active: Shakespeare wrote "Hamlet". → Passive: "Hamlet" ___ by Shakespeare.

9. Active: The company employs 200 people. → Passive: 200 people ___ by the company.

10. Active: The police didn't catch the thief. → Passive: The thief ___ by the police.
