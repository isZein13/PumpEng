Present Perfect Continuous

Упражнения на действия, которые начались в прошлом и продолжаются до сих пор (или только что закончились).

Present Perfect Continuous используется для описания действия, которое началось в прошлом и продолжалось до настоящего момента. Оно делает акцент на длительности процесса. Формула: have / has + been + V-ing.

  • Действие, которое все еще продолжается: "I have been waiting for you for two hours." (Я жду тебя уже два часа. - Я начал ждать в прошлом и все еще жду).
  • Действие, которое только что закончилось, но результат виден сейчас: "Why are you so tired?" - "I have been running." (Почему ты такой уставший? - Я бегал).
  • Часто используется с for (в течение) и since (с какого-то момента).

Упражнение 1: Образование (have/has been + V-ing)

1. I ___ (work) here for five years.

2. She ___ (study) all day.

3. They ___ (play) tennis since this morning.

4. He ___ (not / feel) well recently.

5. We ___ (drive) for hours. Are we there yet?

6. ___ it ___ (rain) all day?

7. How long ___ you ___ (learn) English?

8. My friends ___ (wait) for me for an hour.

9. He ___ (not / watch) TV. He has been reading.

10. I ___ (think) about this problem all morning.

Упражнение 2: Действия, продолжающиеся до настоящего момента

1. He is a great guitarist. He ___ (play) since he was a child.

2. How long ___ you ___ (wait) for the bus? You look cold.

3. They are building a new road. They ___ (work) on it for months.

4. I ___ (try) to call you all morning.

5. She's a teacher. She ___ (teach) for over 20 years.

6. It ___ (snow) since yesterday. Everything is white.

7. We ___ (live) in this apartment since 2018.

8. You ___ (watch) TV for hours. It's time to take a break.

9. How long ___ she ___ (learn) Spanish? Her accent is very good.

10. I ___ (feel) much better since I started exercising.

Упражнение 3: Недавно завершенные действия с результатом

1. Why are your clothes so dirty? - I ___ (work) in the garden.

2. You look exhausted! - I am. I ___ (run) for an hour.

3. The kitchen is a mess! What ___ you ___ (do)?

4. My eyes are tired because I ___ (read) on my tablet for hours.

5. He smells of paint because he ___ (paint) the living room.

6. Her fingers are sore. She ___ (play) the guitar all afternoon.

7. I'm sorry I'm late. ___ you ___ (wait) long?

8. Why is the ground wet? - It ___. (rain)

9. We need to rest. We ___ (walk) for the last three hours.

10. You've got flour on your nose! ___ you ___ (bake)?

Упражнение 4: Использование for и since

Выберите правильный маркер времени: 'for' (период) или 'since' (точка отсчета).

1. They have been talking on the phone ___ two hours.

2. I have been studying English ___ I was a child.

3. He has been waiting ___ 9 o'clock this morning.

4. We have been living here ___ a long time.

5. She hasn't been feeling well ___ last Tuesday.

6. It has been snowing ___ more than a day.

7. How long have you been learning to play the piano? ___ about three years.

8. They have been renovating their house ___ the beginning of summer.

9. I haven't seen her ___ we graduated from university.

10. He has been working on this project ___ ages.

Упражнение 5: Present Perfect Simple vs. Continuous

Выберите правильное время: Simple (акцент на результате) или Continuous (акцент на процессе).

1. I ___ (read) this book for three hours, and I'm still on page 50.

2. I ___ (read) three books by this author so far.

3. She ___ (write) emails all morning. She needs a break.

4. She ___ (write) ten emails today.

5. They ___ (build) that bridge for over a year and it's still not finished.

6. Ouch! I ___ (cut) my finger.

7. How long ___ you ___ (wait) for me?

8. He ___ (lose) his keys again. He can't get into the house.

9. My brother ___ (learn) to drive for six months.

10. I ___ (already / see) that film three times.
