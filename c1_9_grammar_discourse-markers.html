Discourse Markers (Связующие слова)

Упражнения на использование слов и фраз для построения логичной и структурированной речи.

Discourse Markers — это "дорожные знаки" в нашей речи и письме. Они помогают связывать идеи, показывать отношения между ними и направлять слушателя или читателя.

  • Contrast/Concession (Контраст/Уступка): however, nevertheless, on the other hand, although, despite.
  • Addition/Emphasis (Добавление/Усиление): furthermore, moreover, in addition, what's more, indeed.
  • Result/Consequence (Результат/Следствие): therefore, consequently, as a result, thus.
  • Structuring/Sequencing (Структурирование): To begin with, subsequently, finally, in conclusion, overall.

Упражнение 1: Contrast & Concession

1. The plan was excellent. ___, it was too expensive to implement.

2. ___ the heavy rain, they decided to go ahead with the outdoor concert.

3. He is a very talented musician. ___, his brother has no musical ability at all.

4. ___ the warnings from the weather service, they went sailing.

5. The journey was long and tiring. ___, we were glad we made it.

6. ___ he had studied hard, he didn't pass the final exam.

7. City life can be exciting. ___, it can also be very stressful.

8. He knew it was a risky investment. He put all his money into it ___.

9. ___ his lack of experience, he got the job.

10. The flight was delayed for hours. ___, the airline offered no compensation.

Упражнение 2: Addition & Emphasis

1. The new system is more efficient. ___, it is much cheaper to run.

2. The hotel has a swimming pool and a gym. ___, it offers free breakfast.

3. He is an excellent speaker; ___, he is a brilliant writer.

4. She is fluent in three languages. ___, she is learning a fourth.

5. The team played exceptionally well. ___, they were the clear winners.

6. Regular exercise improves physical health. ___, it has significant benefits for mental clarity.

7. He is not just a good student; ___, he is the best in his class.

8. The new app is user-friendly. ___, it has several unique features.

9. The presentation was well-researched. ___, it was delivered with great confidence.

10. I love this city for its vibrant culture. ___, the food is incredible.

Упражнение 3: Result & Consequence

1. He didn't study for the test; ___, he failed.

2. The company invested heavily in new technology. ___, their productivity increased by 20%.

3. The flight was delayed for several hours. ___, we missed our connecting flight.

4. She had not saved any money. ___, she could not afford a vacation.

5. The project was poorly managed and underfunded; ___, its failure was inevitable.

6. The evidence was overwhelming. ___, the jury found him guilty.

7. He forgot to set his alarm. ___, he was late for work.

8. There was a major power outage. ___, all the trains were cancelled.

9. He is a workaholic and rarely takes breaks. ___, his health has suffered.

10. The company lost a major client. ___, they had to lay off several employees.

Упражнение 4: Structuring & Sequencing

1. ___, I'd like to thank everyone for coming. Then, I will outline the main points of our discussion.

2. He worked in London for five years. ___, he moved to New York.

3. ___, it was a very successful event despite a few minor issues.

4. We considered several options. ___, we decided to postpone the project.

5. ___, there are several reasons why this plan is not feasible.

6. First, we need to analyze the data. ___, we can develop a strategy.

7. He was initially hesitant. ___, he became one of the project's biggest supporters.

8. ___, I'd like to summarize the main findings of our research.

9. We discussed the budget, the timeline, and the marketing strategy. ___, we covered all the key aspects.

10. ___, let me introduce my colleague, who will be co-presenting with me today.

2. ___ the high cost of living in the city, many young people are moving to the suburbs.

3. The report highlighted several key issues. ___, it offered a number of practical solutions.

4. He has a very strong alibi; ___, it's unlikely he committed the crime.

5. He studied German for years. ___, he still finds it difficult to have a fluent conversation.

6. The traffic was terrible. ___, he managed to arrive on time.

7. ___ the fact that he has no experience, he seems very confident.

8. ___, let's review the main points before we finish the meeting.

9. She missed her train, and ___ she was late for the interview.
