Participle Clauses (Причастные обороты)

Упражнения на использование причастий для создания более сложных и лаконичных предложений.

Причастные обороты — это способ заменить придаточное предложение, сделав речь более элегантной и краткой. Они относятся к подлежащему главного предложения.

  • Present Participle (-ing): Заменяет активное действие, происходящее одновременно с главным или сразу после него.
    • Feeling tired, I went to bed early. (= Because I felt tired...)
  • Past Participle (-ed/V3): Заменяет пассивное действие.
    • Written in a hurry, the report contained several mistakes. (= Because it was written in a hurry...)
  • Perfect Participle (Having + V3): Заменяет действие, которое произошло до главного действия.
    • Having finished her work, she went home. (= After she had finished her work...)

Упражнение 1: Present Participle Clauses (-ing)

Выберите правильную форму Present Participle.

1. ___ he was tired, he decided to keep working.

2. ___ the door, she stepped into the darkness.

3. ___ that he was late, he started to run.

4. ___ no money, I couldn't buy a ticket.

5. The man ___ on the corner is my English teacher.

6. ___ her name, he looked up in surprise.

7. ___ the risks, she decided to proceed with the plan.

8. The train ___ at platform 2 is going to Liverpool.

9. ___ a loud crash, we rushed outside to see what had happened.

10. ___ she was ill, she came to the meeting.

Упражнение 2: Past Participle (-ed/V3) Clauses

Выберите правильную форму Past Participle.

1. ___ by a famous architect, the building is a city landmark.

2. The man ___ in the accident was taken to hospital.

3. ___ in a hurry, the email contained several typos.

4. The paintings ___ from the gallery have never been recovered.

5. ___ by the loud noise, the baby started to cry.

6. The wallet ___ on the street was handed in to the police.

7. ___ with fresh ingredients, the meal tasted delicious.

8. Most of the guests ___ to the party were from her company.

9. ___ by the beauty of the landscape, he decided to stay longer.

10. The ideas ___ in his book are still relevant today.

Упражнение 3: Perfect Participle Clauses (Having + V3)

1. ___ her work, she went out for a walk.

2. ___ the book, I can now watch the movie.

3. ___ all his money, he had to look for a job.

4. ___ in the company for 20 years, he decided it was time to retire.

5. ___ three times, he didn't want to take the exam again.

6. ___ her keys, she couldn't get into her apartment.

7. ___ the door, he realized he had been robbed.

8. ___ to the city, she felt overwhelmed by the noise and crowds.

9. ___ his lunch, he went back to work.

10. ___ all the necessary preparations, they were ready to start the experiment.

Упражнение 4: Choosing the Correct Participle

1. ___ her keys, she had to call a locksmith.

2. ___ in the 16th century, the castle is a major tourist attraction.

3. ___ for hours, they decided to stop for a rest.

4. The girl ___ the red dress is my sister.

5. ___ about the delay, the passengers became angry.

6. ___ from a distance, the city looks like a tiny model.

7. ___ the whole day travelling, they were desperate for a shower and a good night's sleep.

8. ___ by the director, the actor felt more confident.

9. ___ for the job, she had to prepare for the interview.

10. ___ all their options, they finally made a decision.

Упражнение 5: Sentence Combination

Объедините два предложения в одно, используя причастный оборот.

1. He opened the drawer. He took out a gun.

2. The painting was damaged in the fire. It is now worthless.

3. He had worked in the company for 20 years. He knew everyone.

4. The boy was sitting on the pavement. He was crying.

5. The city was founded by the Romans. It has many ancient ruins.

6. Because she didn't want to miss the train, she ran to the station.

7. The building was abandoned for years. It is now being renovated.

8. He put on his coat. He went out.

9. He was amazed by the view. He couldn't speak.

10. After he had completed the form, he gave it to the receptionist.
