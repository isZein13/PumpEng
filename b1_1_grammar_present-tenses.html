Present Tenses (Review & Advanced Use)

Углубленная практика настоящих времен, включая глаголы состояния и другие нюансы.

На уровне B1 мы углубляем понимание настоящих времен, фокусируясь на глаголах состояния (Stative Verbs). Эти глаголы описывают состояния, чувства, мысли, а не действия, и обычно не используются во временах Continuous.

  • Типичные Stative Verbs: know, believe, understand, remember, want, need, love, like, hate, own, have (в значении "владеть"), see (в значении "понимать"), think (в значении "считать").
  • Смешанные глаголы (Dual Meaning Verbs) могут быть статичными или динамичными в зависимости от контекста:
    • I think this is a good idea. (Мнение - Stative) vs. I'm thinking about my exam. (Процесс - Dynamic)
    • He has a car. (Владение - Stative) vs. He's having lunch. (Процесс - Dynamic)

Упражнение 1: Present Simple vs. Present Continuous (Review)

1. Water ___ (boil) at 100 degrees Celsius.

2. Look! The children ___ (play) in the garden.

3. He usually ___ (work) from home, but this week he ___ (work) from the office.

4. "Where is Maria?" "She ___ (have) a shower."

5. I ___ (not / watch) TV very often.

6. Please be quiet. I ___ (try) to concentrate.

7. How often ___ you ___ (go) to the dentist?

8. The sun ___ (rise) in the east.

9. My parents ___ (live) in a small town.

10. The company's sales ___ (increase) this year.

Упражнение 2: Stative Verbs (Глаголы состояния)

1. I ___ (not / believe) his story. It sounds false.

2. She ___ (want) to buy a new car now.

3. This soup ___ (taste) delicious.

4. He ___ (own) three restaurants in the city.

5. I ___ (understand) the problem now. Thank you.

6. We ___ (need) your help at the moment.

7. This box ___ (contain) old photographs.

8. Do you ___ (know) the answer?

9. He ___ (seem) to be very happy today.

10. I ___ (prefer) tea to coffee.

Упражнение 3: Verbs with Dual Meanings (Смешанные глаголы)

1. I ___ (think) you are right. (мнение)

2. What are you doing? I ___ (think) about my plans for the weekend. (процесс)

3. She ___ (have) a beautiful house. (владение)

4. They ___ (have) a party right now. (действие)

5. The chef ___ (taste) the soup to see if it needs more salt. (действие)

6. This coffee ___ (taste) a bit strange. (качество)

7. I ___ (see) what you mean. (понимаю)

8. He ___ (see) his doctor tomorrow morning. (встречаюсь)

9. These flowers ___ (smell) wonderful. (качество)

10. Why ___ you ___ (smell) the milk? Do you think it's bad? (действие)

Упражнение 4: Present Continuous for Annoying Habits

Выберите правильную форму, чтобы выразить раздражение.

1. He ___ (always / lose) his keys! It's so annoying.

2. She ___ (constantly / interrupt) me when I'm speaking.

3. My neighbors ___ (forever / make) noise at night. I can't sleep.

4. You ___ (always / complain) about your job. Why don't you find a new one?

5. I hate this phone. It ___ (always / ring) when I'm in a meeting.

6. My brother ___ (always / borrow) my clothes without asking!

7. Why ___ you ___ (constantly / ask) me for money?

8. This car is useless. It ___ (always / break) down.

9. He's so forgetful. He ___ (forever / forget) where he parked his car.

10. Stop it! You ___ (always / make) the same mistake.

Упражнение 5: Mixed Practice (Итоговая проверка)

Выберите наиболее подходящую форму глагола.

1. I can't talk now, I ___ (drive).

2. This information ___ (seem) to be incorrect.

3. What ___ (this word / mean)? I don't understand it.

4. My sister ___ (always / leave) her wet towel on the bed!

5. A: What's that smell? B: The chef ___ (taste) the new sauce.

6. He ___ (not / remember) my name. He never does.

7. Why ___ (you / look) at me like that? Is something wrong?

8. It ___ (depend) on the weather whether we go or not.

9. I ___ (think) about moving to a new city, but I'm not sure yet.

10. The Earth ___ (revolve) around the sun.
