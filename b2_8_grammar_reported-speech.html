Reported Speech (Advanced)

Упражнения на передачу сложных утверждений, вопросов и предложений с reporting verbs.

На уровне B2 мы расширяем арсенал глаголов для передачи косвенной речи (reporting verbs), которые требуют после себя герундий или инфинитив, и рассматриваем сложные случаи сдвига модальных глаголов.

  • Reporting Verb + Gerund: `admit`, `deny`, `suggest`. "Let's go to the cinema." → He suggested going to the cinema.
  • Reporting Verb + Infinitive: `promise`, `offer`, `refuse`, `agree`. "I'll help you." → He promised to help me.
  • Reporting Verb + Object + Infinitive: `advise`, `warn`, `remind`. "You should see a doctor." → He advised me to see a doctor.
  • Reporting Modals:
    • `must` (obligation) → `had to`. "I must finish this." → He said he had to finish it.
    • `must` (deduction), `might`, `should`, `ought to` → обычно не меняются. "He must be tired." → She thought he must be tired.

Упражнение 1: Review of Tense Shifts (Complex Tenses)

1. "I am waiting for the bus." → He said he ___ for the bus.

2. "We have finished the project." → They told us they ___ the project.

3. "She was sleeping when I called." → He said she ___ when he had called.

4. "I will be working at 5 PM." → She said she ___ at 5 PM.

5. "He had already left when I arrived." → She said he ___ when she had arrived.

6. "I must go to the bank." → He said he ___ to go to the bank.

7. "They might come later." → I thought they ___ come later.

8. "I have been waiting for an hour." → She complained that she ___ for an hour.

9. "We are going to launch a new product." → The company announced they ___ a new product.

10. "You should see a doctor." → She told me I ___ a doctor.

9. "How long have you been waiting?" → She asked me how long I ___.

Упражнение 4: Reporting Modal Verbs

2. "He must be tired. He's been working all day." → I said that he ___ be tired.

3. "You should see a doctor." → He advised me that I ___ a doctor.

Упражнение 5: Full Sentence Transformation

Выберите единственно верный вариант косвенной речи.

1. "Why didn't you call me yesterday?" he asked her.

2. "I've been working on this project all week," she said.

3. "Don't forget to lock the door," my dad said to me.

4. "Are you going to apply for the job?" he asked.

5. "I must call my parents tonight," he said.

6. "Would you like a cup of tea?" she asked.

7. "I'll never do that again!" he said.

8. "What have you been doing?" she asked.

9. "You should apply for the scholarship," he told her.

10. "Let's postpone the meeting," the manager said.
