Passive Voice (Advanced)

Упражнения на сложные пассивные конструкции с модальными глаголами, инфинитивом и герундием.

На уровне B2 мы расширяем использование пассивного залога на более сложные грамматические структуры.

  • Passive with Modals: Modal + be + V3 (This work must be finished today.)
  • Passive Infinitive: to be + V3 (He wants to be promoted.)
  • Passive Gerund: being + V3 (She hates being told what to do.)
  • Causative Form (Review): have/get + object + V3 (I had my car repaired.)
  • Passive with Reporting Verbs:
    • It is said that he is rich.
    • He is said to be rich.

Упражнение 1: Passive with Modals

1. This problem can ___ (solve) easily.

2. The report must ___ (submit) by 5 PM.

3. The rules should ___ (follow) by everyone.

4. This medicine must not ___ (take) on an empty stomach.

5. The information might ___ (find) on their website.

6. A solution has to ___ (find) soon.

7. The match may ___ (postpone) because of the rain.

8. These documents cannot ___ (copy) without permission.

9. More money ought to ___ (spend) on education.

10. The truth will eventually ___ (reveal).

Упражнение 4: Passive with Reporting Verbs

Преобразуйте предложения с reporting verbs в пассивные конструкции.

1. People say that this is the best restaurant in town. → It ___ that this is the best restaurant.

2. People say that this is the best restaurant in town. → This restaurant ___ to be the best.

3. They believe he is a very rich man. → He ___ to be a very rich man.

4. Everyone knows that he stole the money. → It ___ that he stole the money.

5. They thought that the company was failing. → The company ___ to be failing.

6. People expect that the government will lower taxes. → The government ___ to lower taxes.

7. They reported that two people were injured. → Two people ___ to have been injured.

8. Journalists alleged that the minister had accepted bribes. → The minister ___ to have accepted bribes.

9. People consider him to be an expert. → He ___ to be an expert.

10. It is claimed that the new drug can cure the disease. → The new drug ___ to cure the disease.

Упражнение 5: Mixed Tense Passive Transformation

Выберите правильное пассивное предложение.

1. Active: They are building a new skyscraper downtown.

2. Active: Someone had stolen her car before she called the police.

3. Active: The company will have launched the product by next year.

4. Active: The government has approved new regulations.

5. Active: You should check the details carefully.

6. Active: They were discussing the problem when I entered.

7. Active: Nobody has solved this puzzle yet.

8. Active: He is going to write a report.

9. Active: She had been cleaning the house for hours before they arrived.

10. Active: Someone might have left the door open.
