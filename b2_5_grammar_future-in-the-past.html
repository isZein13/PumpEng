Future in the Past (Будущее в прошлом)

Упражнения на описание событий, которые были будущими с точки зрения прошлого.

Future in the Past используется для описания действия, которое было будущим в определенный момент в прошлом. Это взгляд на будущее из прошлого.

  • would + V1: Самая частая форма, используется для передачи `will` в косвенной речи и для описания будущих событий в прошлом. He said he would call.
  • was/were going to + V1: Используется для планов и намерений в прошлом (аналог `be going to`). I was going to travel, but I got sick.
  • was/were to + V1: Более формальная конструкция для официальных планов и договоренностей. The president was to give a speech.
  • was/were about to + V1: Для действий, которые должны были произойти немедленно. I was about to leave when the phone rang.

Упражнение 1: 'would' for Future in the Past

1. He promised he ___ be on time.

2. I knew that she ___ not like the surprise.

3. They said they ___ help us with the project.

4. I thought you ___ be here an hour ago.

5. She hoped that her friends ___ remember her birthday.

6. Direct speech: "I will call you." → He said he ___ call me.

7. I never imagined that one day I ___ live in New York.

8. He told us the story ___ be very interesting.

9. We were sure that our team ___ win the match.

10. The weather forecast said it ___ snow the next day.

Упражнение 2: 'was/were going to'

Используйте 'was/were going to' для планов и намерений в прошлом.

1. I ___ call you, but I forgot.

2. We ___ travel to Italy, but we had to cancel our trip.

3. He ___ tell her the truth, but he couldn't.

4. I thought they ___ help us, but they were too busy.

5. She ___ start her new job, but the company went bankrupt.

6. I knew you ___ say that!

7. They ___ renovate the kitchen, but they ran out of money.

8. At that moment, I thought I ___ faint.

9. He had a plan. He ___ quit his job and travel the world.

10. I ___ invite her, but I found out she was busy.

Упражнение 4: Formal Structures ('was/were to', 'was/were about to')

Выберите правильную формальную или немедленную конструкцию.

1. The Queen ___ visit the new hospital the following week. (formal plan)

2. I ___ leave when the phone rang. (immediate action)

3. The new law ___ come into effect on January 1st. (official schedule)

4. She ___ say something, but she changed her mind. (immediate action)

5. The conference delegates ___ arrive in two groups. (formal arrangement)

6. We ___ board the plane when a sudden storm hit the airport. (immediate action)

7. The Prime Minister ___ make an important announcement that evening. (formal schedule)

8. He ___ ask her a question, but he saw she was busy. (immediate action)

9. If the plan had gone ahead, they ___ have launched the product in May. (formal plan)

10. The ship ___ leave the harbor when the fog rolled in. (immediate action)
