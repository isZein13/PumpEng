Modal Perfects

Упражнения на предположения, сожаления и критику действий в прошлом.

Modal Perfects (модальный глагол + have + V3) используются для выражения нашего отношения к событиям в прошлом.

  • must have + V3: Сильная уверенность, что что-то произошло. (The ground is wet. It must have rained. - Должно быть, шел дождь.)
  • can't / couldn't have + V3: Сильная уверенность, что что-то не произошло. (He can't have finished the book already. It's too long. - Не может быть, чтобы он уже закончил книгу.)
  • may / might / could have + V3: Предположение, что что-то, возможно, произошло. (She's late. She might have missed the bus. - Возможно, она опоздала на автобус.)
  • should / shouldn't have + V3: Сожаление или критика по поводу прошлого. (I should have studied harder. - Мне следовало учиться усерднее.)

Упражнение 1: Deduction (must have / can't have)

1. The lights are off and nobody is answering the door. They ___ out.

2. You just ate a huge meal! You ___ be hungry already.

3. He passed the exam with a perfect score. He ___ very hard.

4. She said she was in Paris yesterday, but I saw her at the local supermarket. She ___.

5. The streets are covered in snow. It ___ a lot last night.

6. The vase is broken. The cat ___ it over.

7. He was at work all day. He ___ the crime.

8. They look so happy. They ___ some good news.

9. This project is excellent. You ___ a lot of time on it.

10. The cake is gone, but Tom hates chocolate. He ___ it.

Упражнение 2: Regret & Criticism (should have / shouldn't have)

1. I failed the exam. I ___ studied harder.

2. He's angry with me. I ___ said that to him.

3. We missed our flight. We ___ left home earlier.

4. You ___ eaten so much cake. Now you feel sick.

5. I'm sorry I'm late. I ___ called you to let you know.

6. The party was great! You ___ come.

7. It was a secret. You ___ told anyone.

8. He ___ bought such an expensive car. He can't afford it.

9. I feel much better now. I ___ worried so much.

10. You ___ listened to my advice. Now you're in trouble.

Упражнение 3: Possibility (may / might / could have)

Используйте 'may have', 'might have' или 'could have' для предположений о прошлом.

1. I can't find my phone. I ___ left it in the restaurant.

2. She's very late. She ___ missed the train.

3. He's not sure how the window broke. He thinks a bird ___ flown into it.

4. "Why didn't Tom answer?" "He ___ not have heard his phone."

5. The package hasn't arrived. They ___ sent it to the wrong address.

6. It's a dangerous path. You ___ been injured!

7. I don't know why she's not here. She ___ forgotten about our meeting.

8. A: Do you think he passed the test? B: I'm not sure. He ___.

9. He didn't come to work. He ___ been sick.

10. The road is closed. There ___ been an accident.

Упражнение 4: Mixed Practice

Выберите лучший модальный глагол для каждой ситуации.

1. I can't believe it! You ___ eaten the whole cake by yourself.

2. The exam was really difficult. I ___ failed it. I'm so worried.

3. I feel terrible. I ___ eaten that suspicious-looking seafood.

4. She walks with a limp. She ___ injured her leg.

5. You ___ told me you were going to be late! I was waiting for an hour.

6. The package arrived this morning. The sender ___ posted it on Monday.

7. I didn't get the job. I ___ prepared better for the interview.

8. This document is in French, but he doesn't speak it. He ___ understood it.

9. He looks very familiar. We ___ met somewhere before.

10. You ___ bought that expensive watch! We need to save money.

Упражнение 5: Rephrasing Situations

Выберите модальную конструкцию, которая лучше всего описывает ситуацию.

1. Situation: I'm almost sure he took your keys. His are identical.

2. Situation: It was a mistake to tell him the secret. Now everyone knows.

3. Situation: I'm not sure, but it's possible that I left the oven on.

4. Situation: I'm sure she didn't see you. She would have said hello.

5. Situation: It was a bad idea to drive so fast in the rain.

6. Situation: It's possible the train was delayed. That's one explanation.

7. Situation: I'm sure they didn't hear us. The music was too loud.

8. Situation: I regret not buying the tickets earlier. Now they are sold out.

9. Situation: It's the only logical conclusion: he forgot the meeting.

10. Situation: I advise you not to shout at the boss. It was a mistake.
