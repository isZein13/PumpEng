Hypothetical Meaning

Упражнения на конструкции для выражения желаний, сожалений и предпочтений.

Конструкции с гипотетическим значением используются для разговора о нереальных или желаемых ситуациях, используя "сдвиг времени" в прошлое.

  • I wish / If only + Past Simple: Сожаление о ситуации в настоящем.
    • I wish I had more money. (Жаль, что у меня сейчас нет больше денег).
  • I wish / If only + Past Perfect: Сожаление о ситуации в прошлом.
    • If only I had studied harder for the exam. (Если бы только я усерднее готовился к экзамену).
  • I wish + would: Жалоба на повторяющееся действие или желание, чтобы кто-то изменил поведение.
    • I wish you would stop making that noise.
  • It's (high) time + Past Simple: Выражение того, что действие уже давно пора совершить.
    • It's high time you found a job. (Давно пора тебе найти работу).
  • would rather + V1 / (that) + S + Past Simple: Выражение предпочтения.
    • I'd rather stay home. (Я бы предпочел остаться дома).
    • I'd rather you stayed home. (Я бы предпочел, чтобы ты остался дома).

Упражнение 1: 'I wish / If only' for Present/Future

1. I'm so busy. I wish I ___ more free time.

2. It's raining. If only the sun ___.

3. I wish you ___ making so much noise. I can't concentrate.

4. If only I ___ the answer to this question.

5. I wish I ___ taller so I could be a basketball player.

6. If only it ___ Friday today. I'm tired of working.

7. I wish the neighbours ___ their music down. It's so loud.

8. If only we ___ live closer to the sea.

9. I wish I ___ have to work tomorrow.

10. I wish my dog ___ bark every time someone passes the house.

Упражнение 2: 'I wish / If only' for Past

1. I missed the bus. I wish I ___ home earlier.

2. If only I ___ what he said. I feel so bad about it now.

3. She wishes she ___ to university when she was younger.

4. I wish I ___ more photos during my trip last year.

5. If only they ___ us about the problem earlier.

6. He wishes he ___ so much money on that useless gadget.

7. I wish I ___ to my parents' advice. They were right.

8. If only I ___ her phone number before she moved.

9. They wish they ___ that house when it was cheaper.

10. I wish I ___ there to help you yesterday.

Упражнение 3: 'It's time / It's high time'

1. It's almost midnight. It's time we ___ home.

2. You're 30 years old! It's high time you ___ your parents' house.

3. The kitchen is a mess. It's time someone ___ it.

4. Don't you think it's time you ___ apologizing for what you did?

5. It's time the children ___ in bed. It's very late.

6. It's high time the government ___ something about this problem.

7. This car is ancient. It's time we ___ a new one.

8. It's time you ___ taking your responsibilities more seriously.

9. You've been wearing those same clothes for days. It's time you ___ them.

10. It's getting late. Isn't it time we ___?

Упражнение 4: 'would rather'

1. I'm tired. I'd rather ___ at home tonight.

2. I'd rather you ___ smoke in here.

3. Would you rather ___ tea or coffee?

4. He'd rather I ___ him about the problem.

5. I'd rather ___ than work with him.

6. Don't you think you'd rather ___ the doctor about that cough?

7. I'd rather my children ___ so much television.

8. A: "Should we go by bus?" B: "I'd rather ___ a taxi."

9. I'd rather you ___ me the truth last night.

10. He'd rather ___ alone.
