Advanced Adjectives & Adverbs

Упражнения на тонкости употребления прилагательных и наречий, включая коллокации и порядок слов.

На уровне C1 мы оттачиваем использование прилагательных и наречий, фокусируясь на стилистических нюансах и устойчивых выражениях.

  • Gradable vs. Ungradable Adjectives:
    • Gradable (имеющие степени): `hot`, `big`, `interesting`. Усиливаются с `very`, `extremely`, `fairly`.
    • Ungradable (экстремальные): `boiling`, `huge`, `fascinating`. Усиливаются с `absolutely`, `completely`, `utterly`.
  • Position of Adverbs: Позиция наречий (`only`, `even`, `just`) может кардинально менять смысл предложения.
  • Collocations: Устойчивые сочетания, которые звучат естественно для носителей языка (например, `bitterly disappointed`, `fully aware`).

Упражнение 1: Gradable and Ungradable Adjectives

1. The weather was hot, in fact, it was ___ boiling.

2. I was ___ tired after the long journey.

3. The new film is ___ fantastic. You have to see it!

4. Are you sure you can afford that? It looks ___ expensive.

5. The idea is ___ ridiculous. I can't believe he suggested it.

6. The mansion was ___ huge, with over 50 rooms.

7. I'm ___ certain that I left my keys on the table.

8. The exam was ___ difficult, but not impossible.

9. The silence in the ancient forest was ___ total.

10. Her explanation was ___ impossible to believe.

Упражнение 2: Position of Adverbs (Advanced)

Выберите предложение с наиболее естественным и правильным порядком слов.

1. Which sentence means that I didn't see anyone except Tom?

2. Which is the most natural sentence?

3. Which sentence means she wasn't expecting her friends either?

4. Which is the most natural sentence?

5. Which is correct and most common?

6. Which sentence is grammatically correct?

7. Which sentence has a different meaning?

8. Which is the most natural sentence?

9. Which sentence is INCORRECT?

10. Which sentence emphasizes that the WHOLE house was painted, not just a part?

Упражнение 3: Adjective + Preposition Collocations

4. She is responsible ___ managing the entire team.

5. I'm not very good ___ remembering names.

6. This city is famous ___ its historical monuments.

7. I'm so tired ___ hearing your excuses.

8. This product is very similar ___ the one we saw in the other shop.

9. He is capable ___ running a marathon.

10. I am really disappointed ___ you. I thought I could trust you.
