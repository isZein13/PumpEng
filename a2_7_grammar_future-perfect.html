Future Perfect (Будущее совершенное время)

Упражнения на действия, которые завершатся к определенному моменту в будущем.

Future Perfect используется для описания действия, которое начнется и завершится ДО определенного момента или другого действия в будущем. Оно показывает результат к будущей точке. Формула: will have + V3 (третья форма глагола).

  • Ключевые слова-маркеры: by (к какому-то моменту), by the time (к тому времени как).
  • Пример: "By 9 o'clock, I will have finished my homework." (К 9 часам я уже закончу свою домашнюю работу.)
  • С другим действием: "By the time you arrive, we will have eaten dinner." (К тому времени как ты приедешь, мы уже поужинаем.)

Упражнение 1: Образование (will have + V3)

1. By next year, she ___ (graduate) from college.

2. By the time he is 30, he ___ (visit) 20 countries.

3. They ___ (build) the new bridge by next summer.

4. I ___ (read) this book by the end of the week.

5. By 8 PM, we ___ (eat) dinner.

6. By the time the film starts, we ___ (buy) the tickets.

7. In two years' time, I ___ (save) enough money for a car.

8. He ___ (write) his novel by the end of the year.

9. By the time you call, she ___ (leave) the office.

10. By 2030, scientists ___ (find) a cure for the disease.

Упражнение 2: Использование с 'by' и 'by the time'

Выберите правильный вариант, обращая внимание на маркеры времени.

1. I will have finished my work ___ 6 PM.

2. ___ the time she arrives, the party will have started.

3. They will have painted the house ___ the end of the week.

4. She will have learned Spanish ___ she moves to Spain.

5. He will have retired ___ the year 2040.

6. ___ you get home, I will have cooked dinner.

7. I will have read all his books ___ next Christmas.

8. The sun will have set ___ we reach the top of the mountain.

9. They will have been married for 10 years ___ next June.

10. The movie will have already finished ___ we get to the cinema.

Упражнение 3: Вопросы и отрицания

1. ___ you ___ (finish) your report by tomorrow?

2. I ___ (not / save) enough money by the end of this year.

3. ___ she ___ (read) the book by the next lesson?

4. They ___ (not / arrive) by the time the meeting starts.

5. How many pages ___ you ___ (write) by midnight?

6. He ___ (not / eat) anything by the time he gets home.

7. ___ they ___ (build) the school by 2025?

8. We ___ (not / finish) painting the house by Saturday.

9. What ___ she ___ (decide) by the next meeting?

10. I'm afraid the package ___ (not / arrive) by your birthday.

6. She is writing a book. She thinks she ___ it by the end of the year.

7. He is saving money. By the end of this year, he ___ enough for a deposit on a house.

8. Don't worry, the mechanic ___ your car by this evening.

9. Let's meet at 6. The rain ___ by then.
