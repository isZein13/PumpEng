Mixed Conditionals (Смешанные условные предложения)

Упражнения на ситуации, где условие и результат относятся к разным временам.

Mixed Conditionals комбинируют части условных предложений 2-го и 3-го типов. Они описывают нереальные ситуации, где условие и результат относятся к разным временным рамкам.

  • Тип 1 (Нереальное прошлое → Нереальное настоящее): If + Past Perfect, ... would + V1.
    • If I had taken that job, I would be rich now. (Если бы я получил ту работу [в прошлом], я бы сейчас был богат [в настоящем]).
  • Тип 2 (Нереальное настоящее → Нереальное прошлое): If + Past Simple, ... would have + V3.
    • If I weren't so shy, I would have talked to him at the party yesterday. (Если бы я не был таким стеснительным [вообще, в настоящем], я бы поговорил с ним на вечеринке вчера [в прошлом]).

Упражнение 1: Review (Type 2 vs. Type 3)

Вспомните "чистые" типы условных предложений.

1. If I ___ you, I would apologize. (Нереальное настоящее - совет)

2. If I ___ you were coming, I would have baked a cake. (Нереальное прошлое)

3. We would go to the beach if the weather ___ better. (Нереальное настоящее)

4. He ___ the exam if he had studied. (Нереальное прошлое)

5. What would you do if you ___ a million euros? (Нереальное настоящее)

6. If they ___ the invitation, they would have come to the party. (Нереальное прошлое)

7. I would learn to play the guitar if I ___ more time. (Нереальное настоящее)

8. She wouldn't have been so tired if she ___ to bed earlier last night. (Нереальное прошлое)

9. If I ___ the president, I would change many things. (Нереальное настоящее)

10. If you had listened to me, you ___ that mistake. (Нереальное прошлое)

Упражнение 2: Mixed Conditional (Past → Present)

1. If I ___ (study) harder last year, I ___ (have) a better job now.

2. If you ___ (not / spend) all your money, you ___ (be) able to buy a coffee now.

3. He ___ (be) very tired today if he ___ (not / sleep) well last night.

4. If she ___ (marry) him, she ___ (be) a queen now.

5. I ___ (not / be) so stressed now if I ___ (finish) my work yesterday.

6. If they ___ (buy) that house, they ___ (have) a huge mortgage now.

7. If I ___ (not / break) my leg, I ___ (go) on a ski trip with you now.

8. We ___ (be) lost now if we ___ (not / ask) for directions.

9. If he ___ (invest) in that company, he ___ (be) a millionaire now.

10. I ___ (know) the answer if I ___ (read) the book.

Упражнение 3: Mixed Conditional (Present → Past)

1. If I ___ (be) a better cook, I ___ (bake) a cake for your birthday yesterday.

2. If he ___ (not / be) so lazy, he ___ (finish) his project on time last week.

3. I ___ (go) to the party yesterday if I ___ (not have to) work today.

4. If she ___ (speak) Russian, she ___ (understand) them at the meeting last Monday.

5. If I ___ (not / be) afraid of spiders, I ___ (help) you remove that one from the bathroom.

6. We ___ (buy) you a present if we ___ (know) it was your birthday.

7. If he ___ (be) more responsible, he ___ (not / forget) our anniversary yesterday.

8. If I ___ (have) a car, I ___ (give) you a ride home last night.

9. They ___ (not / get) lost if they ___ (can) read a map.

10. If he ___ (be) a good friend, he ___ (help) you when you needed it.

Упражнение 4: All Types Mixed

Определите тип условного предложения (2, 3 или смешанный) и выберите правильный вариант.

1. If I had known about the traffic, I ___ a different route.

2. If I were you, I ___ that old car. It looks unreliable.

3. I would be a doctor now if I ___ to medical school.

4. If she spoke better English, she ___ the job last year.

5. What would you have done if you ___ me at the concert?

6. If he weren't so indecisive, he ___ her to marry him ages ago.

7. If I ___ about the meeting, I would be there now.

8. If you ___ so much money on clothes, you could afford a holiday.

9. I would have helped you yesterday if I ___ so busy.

10. If I ___ that you were a vegetarian, I would have made a salad.
