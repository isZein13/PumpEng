Articles (Advanced Usage)

Упражнения на сложные случаи употребления артиклей с географическими и абстрактными понятиями.

На уровне B2 мы рассматриваем более тонкие правила употребления артиклей, выходящие за рамки базовых "a/an - один из" и "the - конкретный".

  • Географические названия:
    • Без артикля (Zero Article): Большинство стран (France, Spain), городов, улиц, озер (Lake Baikal), отдельных гор (Mount Everest).
    • С 'the': Страны во мн.ч. (the Netherlands), с 'Republic', 'Kingdom', 'States' (the USA). Группы островов (the Bahamas), горные цепи (the Alps), реки (the Thames), моря и океаны (the Atlantic), пустыни (the Sahara).
  • Абстрактные и неисчисляемые существительные:
    • В общем значении - без артикля: Life is beautiful. History repeats itself.
    • При конкретизации - с 'the': The life of a soldier is hard. The history of Rome is fascinating.
  • Устойчивые выражения: Во многих выражениях артикль опускается: at home, go to bed, by car, in prison.

Упражнение 1: Артикли с географическими названиями

1. We went on a trip to ___ Rocky Mountains.

2. My brother lives in ___ United Kingdom.

3. They crossed ___ Sahara Desert on camels.

4. I would love to visit ___ Spain one day.

5. ___ Lake Superior is one of the largest freshwater lakes in the world.

6. The ship sank in ___ Pacific Ocean.

7. We climbed ___ Mount Fuji when we were in Japan.

8. They took a cruise down ___ River Nile.

9. Have you ever been to ___ Netherlands?

10. Last year we visited ___ Rome and ___ Vatican City.

Упражнение 2: Абстрактные и неисчисляемые существительные

1. I believe that ___ education is very important.

2. ___ education she received at that university was excellent.

3. ___ happiness is something that everyone seeks.

4. She described ___ happiness she felt on her wedding day.

5. I need ___ advice on this matter.

6. ___ advice you gave me was very helpful.

7. ___ knowledge is power.

8. He has ___ deep knowledge of ancient history.

9. ___ weather was terrible yesterday.

10. We can't go sailing in ___ such bad weather.

Упражнение 3: Zero Article в устойчивых выражениях

1. I usually go to ___ work by ___ bus.

2. He's still at ___ school. He will be back for ___ dinner.

3. She's been in ___ hospital since ___ last Monday.

4. I'm going to ___ bed now. I'm tired.

5. He was sent to ___ prison for five years.

6. I went to ___ university in Moscow.

7. My mother went to ___ prison to visit my uncle.

8. What time do you have ___ breakfast?

9. It's time to go to ___ work.

10. The children are playing in ___ garden.

Упражнение 4: 'a/an' vs. 'the' (Специфические случаи)

1. My sister is ___ doctor.

2. ___ Moon goes around ___ Earth.

3. I play ___ piano, but I can't play ___ football.

4. She is ___ most intelligent person I know.

5. I have an appointment with the doctor twice ___ month.

6. ___ President of the United States will give a speech tonight.

7. I need to buy ___ new pair of shoes.

8. ___ rich should help ___ poor.

9. It was ___ honour to meet him.

10. My son wants to be ___ astronaut when he grows up.

Упражнение 5: Mixed Practice (Все сложные случаи)

1. ___ life would be very different without ___ technology.

2. We flew over ___ Alps on our way to ___ Italy.

3. ___ dinner that you cooked last night was amazing.

4. She has ___ good understanding of ___ physics.

5. I saw ___ accident this morning. ___ car hit ___ tree.

6. He is studying ___ history of ___ ancient Greece.

7. ___ Queen will open ___ new hospital next week.

8. ___ Smiths have a son and a daughter. ___ son is in my class.

9. ___ beauty is subjective, but ___ beauty of this painting is undeniable.

10. He spent a year in ___ prison, and when he got out, his life was never ___ same again.
