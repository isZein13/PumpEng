Indirect Speech (Команды и Просьбы)

Упражнения на передачу команд, приказов и вежливых просьб в косвенной речи.

В этой теме мы рассмотрим, как передавать в косвенной речи команды (commands) и просьбы (requests). В отличие от утверждений, здесь не происходит сдвига времени глагола.

  • Структура: Reporting Verb (tell, ask, order...) + Object (me, him, them...) + (not) to + Infinitive.
  • Положительная команда:
    • Direct: "Close the door, please."
    • Indirect: She asked me to close the door.
  • Отрицательная команда:
    • Direct: "Don't be late."
    • Indirect: The teacher told us not to be late.

Упражнение 1: Положительные команды (tell/ask ... TO do)

1. "Open your books." → The teacher told the students ___ their books.

2. "Please help me." → He asked her ___ him.

3. "Sit down." → She told the dog ___.

4. "Call me later." → He asked me ___ him later.

5. "Be quiet." → The librarian told the children ___ quiet.

6. "Do your homework." → My mom told me ___ my homework.

7. "Turn off the lights." → Dad told us ___ the lights.

8. "Could you pass the salt?" → She asked him ___ the salt.

9. "Clean your room." → My parents told me ___ my room.

10. "Wait here." → The guard told them ___ there.

Упражнение 2: Отрицательные команды (tell/ask ... NOT TO do)

1. "Don't shout." → She told the children ___.

2. "Don't be late." → The teacher told us ___.

3. "Please don't touch that." → He asked me ___ that.

4. "Don't forget your keys." → She reminded him ___ his keys.

5. "Don't eat in the library." → The librarian told them ___ in the library.

6. "Don't worry about it." → She told me ___ about it.

7. "Please don't be noisy." → We asked the neighbors ___.

8. "Don't open the window." → My dad told me ___ the window.

9. "Don't run in the corridor." → The teacher told the children ___.

10. "Don't give up." → My coach told me ___.

Упражнение 3: Смешанная практика команд

Выберите правильную форму: 'to do' или 'not to do'.

1. "Don't talk to me." → He told me ___ to him.

2. "Finish your meal." → His mom told him ___ his meal.

3. "Don't be afraid." → She told the child ___.

4. "Please wait outside." → The doctor asked me ___ outside.

5. "Try again later." → They advised us ___ again later.

6. "Don't make a mess." → I told my kids ___ a mess.

7. "Pay attention." → The instructor told everyone ___.

8. "Don't feed the animals." → The zookeeper warned us ___ the animals.

9. "Write your name here." → The receptionist asked me ___ my name there.

10. "Don't drive so fast." → The passenger asked the driver ___.

Упражнение 4: Команды или Утверждения?

Выберите правильную трансформацию для прямой речи.

1. "I am tired." → He said that he ___ tired.

2. "Be quiet." → He told me ___ quiet.

3. "She works in a bank." → He told me that she ___ in a bank.

4. "Don't work so hard." → She told him ___ so hard.

5. "We went home early." → They said that they ___ home early.

6. "Go home early." → The boss told us ___ home early.

7. "I didn't see him." → She said she ___ him.

8. "Don't forget your coat." → My mom told me ___ my coat.

9. "I'm not hungry." → He said he ___ hungry.

10. "Don't be silly." → She told her friend ___ silly.
