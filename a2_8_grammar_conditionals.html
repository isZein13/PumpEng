Conditionals (Условные предложения)

Упражнения на факты, реальные возможности и гипотетические ситуации.

Conditionals (Условные предложения) описывают результат определенного условия. Они состоят из двух частей: условия (if-clause) и результата (main clause).

  • Conditional Type 0 (Факты): If + Present Simple, ... Present Simple.
    • If you heat ice, it melts. (Если нагревать лед, он тает.)
  • Conditional Type 1 (Реальная возможность в будущем): If + Present Simple, ... will + V1.
    • If it rains tomorrow, we will stay home. (Если завтра пойдет дождь, мы останемся дома.)
  • Conditional Type 2 (Нереальная/гипотетическая ситуация в настоящем/будущем): If + Past Simple, ... would + V1.
    • If I had a lot of money, I would buy a big house. (Если бы у меня было много денег, я бы купил большой дом.)

Упражнение 1: Zero Conditional (Тип 0)

1. If you ___ (heat) water to 100 degrees, it ___ (boil).

2. If babies ___ (be) hungry, they ___ (cry).

3. Plants ___ (die) if they ___ (not get) enough water.

4. If I ___ (drink) too much coffee, I ___ (get) a headache.

5. The ground ___ (get) wet if it ___ (rain).

6. If you ___ (mix) red and blue, you ___ (get) purple.

7. When the sun ___ (go) down, it ___ (get) dark.

8. If you ___ (not / eat), you ___ (become) weak.

9. My teacher gets angry if I ___ (not do) my homework.

10. If you ___ (put) food in the freezer, it ___ (freeze).

Упражнение 2: First Conditional (Тип 1)

1. If you ___ (study) hard, you ___ (pass) the exam.

2. If it ___ (rain) tomorrow, we ___ (not go) to the park.

3. I ___ (call) you if I ___ (have) time.

4. If she ___ (be) late, we ___ (start) without her.

5. You ___ (get) cold if you ___ (not wear) a coat.

6. What ___ you ___ (do) if you ___ (miss) the train?

7. If my sister ___ (pass) her exam, my parents ___ (be) very happy.

8. We ___ (go) on holiday if we ___ (save) enough money.

9. If he ___ (not hurry), he ___ (be) late for work.

10. She ___ (be) upset if you ___ (forget) her birthday.

Упражнение 3: Second Conditional (Тип 2)

1. If I ___ (have) a million dollars, I ___ (buy) a big house.

2. If I ___ (be) you, I ___ (not do) that.

3. What ___ you ___ (do) if you ___ (win) the lottery?

4. If she ___ (live) in a big city, she ___ (have) more opportunities.

5. I ___ (travel) more if I ___ (have) more free time.

6. If he ___ (be) taller, he ___ (can) be a basketball player.

7. We ___ (be) happier if we ___ (not live) in this cold climate.

8. If you ___ (can) fly, where ___ you ___ (go)?

9. They ___ (visit) us more often if they ___ (not be) so busy.

10. If I ___ (know) his number, I ___ (call) him.

Упражнение 4: First или Second Conditional?

Прочитайте ситуацию и выберите правильный тип условного предложения.

1. (A real possibility) If I ___ (see) him tomorrow, I ___ (tell) him the news.

2. (A hypothetical situation) If I ___ (be) a superhero, I ___ (help) everyone.

3. (An imaginary situation) I'm not a bird. But if I ___ (have) wings, I ___ (fly) to the moon.

4. (A real plan) We ___ (go) to the beach if the weather ___ (be) nice on Sunday.

5. (Giving advice) If I ___ (be) you, I ___ (talk) to your boss.

6. (A dream) If I ___ (live) anywhere in the world, I ___ (choose) Italy.

7. (A warning) The dog ___ (bite) you if you ___ (touch) it.

8. (A hypothetical question) What ___ (you / do) if you ___ (find) a wallet full of money?

9. (A real condition) If my team ___ (win) this game, they ___ (be) the champions.

10. (Imagining a different reality) If people ___ (not have) cars, the air ___ (be) cleaner.
