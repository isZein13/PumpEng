Past Simple (Прошедшее простое время)

Упражнения на завершенные действия в прошлом с указанием времени или без него.

Past Simple используется для описания действий, которые начались и закончились в определенный момент в прошлом. Часто используются слова-маркеры: yesterday, last week/month/year, ... ago, in 1990.

  • Для **правильных глаголов** добавляется окончание -ed (play - played, watch - watched).
  • **Неправильные глаголы** имеют свою особую вторую форму (V2), которую нужно запомнить (go - went, see - saw, eat - ate).
  • Глагол 'to be'**: was (для I, he, she, it), were (для you, we, they).

Отрицания: did + not (didn't) + V1 (начальная форма глагола). (I didn't play. She didn't go.) Для 'to be': was/were + not (wasn't, weren't).
Вопросы: Did + подлежащее + V1? (Did you play? Did he go?) Для 'to be': Was/Were + подлежащее? (Was he tired?)

Упражнение 1: Правильные глаголы (+ed) - Выберите правильную форму

1. I ___ (to watch) an interesting film yesterday.

2. She ___ (to visit) her grandparents last week.

3. We ___ (to play) tennis two days ago.

4. He ___ (to clean) his room this morning.

5. They ___ (to talk) about their holidays.

6. You ___ (to help) me a lot yesterday.

7. My mother ___ (to cook) dinner for us.

8. The students ___ (to listen) to the teacher carefully.

9. It ___ (to rain) all day yesterday.

10. We ___ (to walk) in the park for two hours.

Упражнение 2: Неправильные глаголы (V2) - Выберите правильную форму

1. I ___ (to go) to London last year.

2. She ___ (to see) a famous actor yesterday.

3. We ___ (to have) a big breakfast this morning.

4. He ___ (to make) a cake for my birthday.

5. They ___ (to come) to the party late.

6. You ___ (to eat) all the cookies!

7. My teacher ___ (to give) us a lot of homework.

8. I ___ (to take) many photos on my holiday.

9. He ___ (to find) his keys under the sofa.

10. We ___ (to buy) a new TV last month.

Упражнение 3: Отрицания (didn't + V1) - Выберите правильную форму

1. I ___ (not / watch) TV yesterday.

2. She ___ (not / go) to the party last night.

3. We ___ (not / play) computer games.

4. He ___ (not / finish) his homework.

5. They ___ (not / see) the new film.

6. You ___ (not / call) me yesterday.

7. My cat ___ (not / eat) its food this morning.

8. I ___ (not / understand) the question.

9. He ___ (not / buy) any souvenirs.

10. We ___ (not / have) a good time at the concert.

Упражнение 6: Past Simple или Present Simple?

1. I usually ___ (walk) to work, but yesterday I ___ (take) the bus.

2. She ___ (visit) her aunt every Sunday. Last Sunday she ___ (not visit) her.

3. They ___ (play) basketball now. They ___ (play) volleyball yesterday. (PC в первой части, но здесь для упрощения A1 сравниваем только PS и PastS)

4. What ___ you ___ (do) every morning? What ___ you ___ (do) yesterday morning?

5. He ___ (not like) coffee, but he ___ (try) it last week.

6. We always ___ (have) dinner at 7 PM. Yesterday we ___ (have) dinner at 8 PM.

7. ___ she ___ (go) to the cinema often? Yes, she ___ (go) last night.

8. I ___ (not understand) this rule now. I ___ (not understand) it yesterday either.

9. My cat ___ (sleep) a lot every day. Yesterday it ___ (sleep) for 10 hours!

10. ___ they usually ___ (eat) fish on Fridays? Yes, they ___ (eat) fish last Friday.

Упражнение 7: Past Simple, Present Simple или Present Continuous?

1. Look! The children ___ (play) in the snow. They ___ (love) winter.

2. I ___ (not / watch) TV last night because I ___ (be) very tired. I usually ___ (watch) TV in the evenings.

3. "What ___ you ___ (do) now?" "I ___ (make) a cup of tea."

4. She ___ (go) to Italy every summer. Last summer, she ___ (visit) Rome. Now, she ___ (plan) her next trip.

5. We ___ (not / usually / eat) fast food, but yesterday we ___ (have) burgers.

6. "___ it ___ (rain) now?" "No, but it ___ (rain) heavily this morning."

7. My brother ___ (study) for his exams at the moment. He ___ (study) very hard every day.

8. I ___ (see) a good film last night. ___ you often ___ (go) to the cinema?

9. "What ___ your parents ___ (do)?" "They are doctors. They ___ (work) at the local hospital."

10. He ___ (call) me an hour ago. He ___ (wait) for me at the station now.
