Reduced Relative Clauses

Упражнения на сокращение относительных предложений с помощью причастий.

Reduced Relative Clauses — это способ сделать речь более лаконичной, убрав относительное местоимение (who, which, that) и вспомогательный глагол `to be`.

  • Активный залог: `who/which/that + is/are/was/were + V-ing` → V-ing
    • The man who is talking to Ann is my boss. → The man talking to Ann is my boss.
  • Пассивный залог: `who/which/that + is/are/was/were + V3` → V3
    • The book that was written by him is a bestseller. → The book written by him is a bestseller.
  • Сокращать можно только defining relative clauses, в которых относительное местоимение является подлежащим.

Упражнение 1: Present Participle (-ing) Clauses

Замените полную конструкцию сокращенной с причастием на -ing.

1. The woman who is sitting on the bench is feeding the pigeons.

2. Do you know the boy who is playing the piano?

3. The road that joins the two villages is very narrow.

4. The people who are waiting for the bus are getting wet.

5. A train that is carrying soldiers arrived at the station.

6. Students who get high grades will receive a scholarship.

7. The family who lives in the house next door is very noisy.

8. The company that produces these cars has a good reputation.

9. I saw a man who was trying to break into a car.

10. The police want to interview anyone who witnessed the accident.

Упражнение 2: Past Participle (-ed/V3) Clauses

Замените полную конструкцию сокращенной с причастием прошедшего времени.

1. The book that was published last year became a bestseller.

2. The letters which were sent yesterday arrived this morning.

3. Most of the goods that are made in this factory are exported.

4. The car that was stolen from the street has been found.

5. The information which is given in this report is incorrect.

6. The photos which were taken at the wedding are beautiful.

7. This is a list of candidates who were invited for an interview.

8. The meal that was served on the plane was terrible.

9. The man who was arrested by the police has confessed.

10. The vegetables that are sold at this market are very fresh.

Упражнение 3: Choosing the Correct Participle (-ing vs. -ed)

Выберите правильное причастие: -ing для активного действия, -ed/V3 для пассивного.

1. The boy ___ next to me on the plane was very nervous.

2. The information ___ in that article is out of date.

3. Who is that man ___ by the window?

4. The paintings ___ from the museum haven't been found yet.

5. The company ___ these phones is based in California.

6. The new law ___ last month has caused a lot of controversy.

7. The woman ___ the company is a very dynamic leader.

8. The bridge ___ by the storm has now been repaired.

9. Most of the people ___ to the party couldn't come.

10. The train ___ at platform 5 is the one to London.

Упражнение 4: Sentence Transformation

Выберите правильное сокращенное предложение.

1. The film, which was directed by Tarantino, is a masterpiece.

2. The scientists who are researching a cure for cancer are making progress.

3. Anyone who wants to come is welcome.

4. The cake which was made by my mother was delicious.

5. The road that leads to the coast is currently closed.

6. The suspect who was questioned by the police denied everything.

7. The ideas which are presented in this book are fascinating.

8. The girl who was waving at you is my sister.

9. Most of the politicians who were elected have broken their promises.

10. The new law will affect all people who live in the city.

Упражнение 5: Mixed Practice in Context

Заполните пропуски правильной формой причастия.

1. The police are investigating the cars ___ (steal) last night.

2. Who was that girl ___ to your brother at the party?

3. Half of the people ___ to the event didn't show up.

4. I live in a house ___ in the 19th century.

5. The road ___ to the airport is currently closed for repairs.

6. Not all the people ___ on the list have been contacted yet.

7. The ideas ___ in his speech were quite radical.

8. There's a path at the end of this street ___ to the river.

9. Most of the suggestions ___ during the meeting were not very practical.

10. The man ___ by a bee was taken to the hospital.
